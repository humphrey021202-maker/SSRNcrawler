Swiss Vat Act Revision: Upcoming Changes

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 34 (2023), Issue 2, Pages 79-81

Posted: 26 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Christoph M. Meier

Independent

Abstract

The Swiss VAT Act will soon be amended. The amended provisions, if approved, would enter into effect on 1 January 2025. In this article, the author provides an overview of the changes that are likely to have an impact on non-Swiss companies.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2442dhs

Keywords: VAT Act, domestic tax law, platform economy, digital economy, SME
