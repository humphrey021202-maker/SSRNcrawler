Mandatory Binding Tax Arbitration: From Sovereign Rights to Sovereign Duties

European Taxation: Vol. 62 (2022), Issue 4, Pages 179-183

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Sholpan Issakova

Independent

Abstract

In this note, the author clarifies the position of Kazakhstan on tax arbitration as a supplementary dispute resolution mechanism in light of the recent peer review results under Action 14 of the OECD/G20 BEPS Project.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2cqnwz

Keywords: BEPS Project (OECD), dispute resolution, arbitration, tax treaty, tax sovereignty, constitutional law

Suggested Citation:
