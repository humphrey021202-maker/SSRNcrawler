Removing or Impeaching the New York State Comptroller

16 Pages Posted: 21 Nov 2009

Bennett Liebman

Albany Law School

Date Written: June 12, 2009

Abstract

The article explains the laws and options in New York State governing the removal of the State Comptroller. It also explains how a vacancy in the office of the State Comptroller would be filled.

Keywords: State Comptroller, New York State, Impeachment, Removal of Public Officer

Suggested Citation:

Liebman, Bennett, Removing or Impeaching the New York State Comptroller (June 12, 2009). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1418697 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.1418697
