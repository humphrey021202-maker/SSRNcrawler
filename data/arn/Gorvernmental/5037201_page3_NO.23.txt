Fiat State Aid Coming to a Positive End?

International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 29 (2022), Issue 3, Pages 216-219

Posted: 2 Dec 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Marc Rasch

Independent

P. Wroblewski

Independent

Abstract

This article discusses the Opinion of Advocate General Pikamäe in Fiat Chrysler Finance Europe v. Commission (Case C-885/19 P), in particular the considerations whereby the Advocate General concluded that this is not a State aid case.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2es9z7w

Keywords: transfer pricing, ECJ case law, State aid, advance tax ruling, intercompany loan, arm's length principle

Suggested Citation:

Rasch, Marc and Wroblewski, P., Fiat State Aid Coming to a Positive End?. International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 29 (2022), Issue 3, Pages 216-219, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5037201 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/2es9z7w
