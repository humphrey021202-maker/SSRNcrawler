Reform of the Control Concept Under the German Cfc Rules: Problem Areas and Open Questions

European Taxation: Vol. 62 (2022), Issue 12, Pages 544-551

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Till Moser

Independent

S. Hentschel

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors provide an overview of amendments made to the German controlled foreign company (CFC) regulations by the ATAD Implementation Act. In particular, the article focuses on the new definition of ?controlling interest? and certain key problem areas and questions that remain unanswered despite the reform.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/gspch3

Keywords: ATAD, CFC, control, tax reform, ECJ case law

Suggested Citation:
