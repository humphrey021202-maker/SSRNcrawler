Planning for Border Adjustments: A Practical Analysis

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 71 (2017), no. 6a (special issue) ; 10 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/32a4xk

Posted: 11 Apr 2024

David P. Hariton

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: June 26, 2017

Abstract

In this article, the author explains why he thinks the border adjustments proposed by the House Republicans' blueprint will be much more complicated to implement than many people realize.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: tax reform, border tax adjustment, tangible property, transfer pricing, intangibles, services, export

JEL Classification: K33, K34
