Current State of Blockchain and Cryptocurrency for International Governments

163 Pages Posted: 30 Jan 2023

William Riggs

University of San Francisco - School of Management

Vipul Vyas

University of San Francisco - School of Management

Date Written: December 31, 2022

Abstract

Blockchain and cryptocurrency technology have been gaining traction as a form of digital currency, with various countries taking different approaches to its regulation and use. This analysis provides an updated overview of the current state of cryptocurrency in several countries, including the legal and regulatory framework, as well as the level of adoption and acceptance among businesses and consumers. The analysis also covers any recent developments or changes in government policies towards cryptocurrency. Overall, the analysis suggests that while some countries are embracing cryptocurrency and actively promoting its use, others are taking a more cautious approach and implementing stricter regulations.

Keywords: cryptocurrency, blockchain, international, government

JEL Classification: P00, 031
