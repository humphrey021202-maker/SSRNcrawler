Technological Advances in Administering Taxes: Will There Be a Tax Office in the Future?

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 74 (2020), Issue 10, Pages 598-607

Posted: 4 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Dmitri Jegorov

Independent

Abstract

This article provides examples of how modern technologies can contribute to the smooth running of a tax administration, and addresses factors, both internal and external, that enable technology to thrive in such a setting, highlighting the specific journey of the Estonian tax authorities.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2n3wx3e

Keywords: information technology, automation, tax authorities, tax technology, digital economy, individual income tax, tax return, tax compliance, exchange of information, recovery of tax

Suggested Citation:
