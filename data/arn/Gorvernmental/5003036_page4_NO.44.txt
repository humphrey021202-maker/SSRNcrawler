An Analysis of the Efficiency of Tax Administrations of 26 European Countries in 2017

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 2, Pages 67-83

Posted: 4 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Athanasios Anastasiou

Independent

C. Kalligosfyris

Independent

E. Kalamara

Independent

Abstract

This article analyses the efficiency of the tax administrations of 26 European countries in 2017. The results showed significant differences between the countries examined, thereby providing useful information regarding the performance of their tax authorities in critical areas.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1416efw

Keywords: tax authorities, collection of tax, electronic filing, audit, tax compliance

Suggested Citation:
