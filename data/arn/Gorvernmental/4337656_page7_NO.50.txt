Current State of Blockchain and Cryptocurrency for Major US Cities

170 Pages Posted: 30 Jan 2023

William Riggs

University of San Francisco - School of Management

Vipul Vyas

University of San Francisco - School of Management

Date Written: December 31, 2022

Abstract

This analysis provides an overview of the current state of blockchain and cryptocurrency in several major cities in the United States, including the legal and regulatory framework, as well as the level of adoption and acceptance among businesses and consumers. The analysis covers cities such as New York City, San Francisco, Chicago, Los Angeles, and Miami, and examines any recent developments or changes in government policies towards cryptocurrency in these areas. The results show that while some cities, particularly those with a strong technology sector, have a high level of cryptocurrency adoption and acceptance, others have yet to fully embrace the technology. Additionally, the analysis highlights that different cities may have varying regulations and policies regarding cryptocurrency usage and trade. Overall, the analysis provides a comprehensive look at the current state of cryptocurrency in several major cities in the United States.

Keywords: cryptocurrency, blockchain, United States, local government, cities

JEL Classification: P00, 031
