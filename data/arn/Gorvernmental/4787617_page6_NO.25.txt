Brazil's Treaty Policy

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 71 (2017), no. 6 (special issue) ; p. 333-339; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/1ye6yy1

Posted: 11 Apr 2024

Sergio André Rocha

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: May 22, 2017

Abstract

The author, in this article, considers Brazil's treaty policy as it has developed over time, with special reference to the influence of the OECD Model and the UN Model on this policy.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: OECD Model, treaty policy, UN Model

JEL Classification: K33, K34
