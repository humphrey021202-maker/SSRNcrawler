Comments on Klaus Vogel Lecture 2021: Unbundled Tax Sovereignty: Refining the Challenges

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 76 (2022), Issue 7, Pages 329-332

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Will H. Morris

Independent

Abstract

In these comments, Will Morris responds positively to Tsilly Dagan's Klaus Vogel Lecture of 2021, agreeing that a new social contract for tax is a way to address some of the problems that globalization has brought about as a result of the interactions between states and their constituents.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/hsyqyd

Keywords: tax sovereignty, globalization, tax morale

Suggested Citation:
