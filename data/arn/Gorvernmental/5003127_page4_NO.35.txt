Japan's Corporate Income Tax: 1995-2021

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 11/12, Pages 715-724

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Yoshihiro Masui

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author provides his personal view of the evolution of Japan's corporate income tax between 1995 and 2021. The year 1995 was taken as the starting point of his story because it was in 1995 that he first became involved with the Bulletin for International Taxation.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/31r94r

Keywords: corporate income tax, tax policy, reorganization, group treatment, SME, international taxation
