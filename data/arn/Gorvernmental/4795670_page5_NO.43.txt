Curbing Base Erosion Via Withholding Taxes: The Case for a 'Reverse Controlled Foreign Company' Approach

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 10 ; p. 578-581; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/2nh1j73

Posted: 29 May 2024

Guglielmo Maisto

Independent

Paolo Arginelli

Independent

Cesare Silvani

Independent

Date Written: September 6, 2018

Abstract

The authors, in this article, propose a "reverse controlled foreign company" withholding tax as a way to counter base erosion.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: base erosion, withholding tax, CFC

JEL Classification: K33, K34
