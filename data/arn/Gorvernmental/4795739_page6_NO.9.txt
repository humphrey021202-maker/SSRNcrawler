Japanese Tax Reforms Square Up to BEPS Action 1 to Tackle Tax Challenges of the Growing Digital Economy

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 4a (Special issue) ; 11 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/4dx3m0

Posted: 25 Apr 2024

Adam Becker

Independent

Date Written: March 26, 2018

Abstract

In this article, the author focuses on Japan's response to the tax challenges of the growing digital economy, including the country's significant reforms in respect of the definition of permanent establishment (PE), the application of consumption tax rules and the reach of anti-tax haven rules to controlled foreign corporations of Japanese-headquartered MNEs.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: digital economy, PE, digital PE, sharing economy, consumption tax, anti-tax haven rules, CFC

JEL Classification: K33, K34
