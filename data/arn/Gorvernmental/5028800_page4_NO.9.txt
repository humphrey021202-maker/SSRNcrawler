2022 State Budget: Economic Recovery of Families Top Priority

European Taxation: Vol. 62 (2022), Issue 9, Pages 401-403

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Rita Botelho Moniz

Independent

Abstract

In this note, the author discusses the drama leading up to approval of the 2022 State Budget, which is focused on the economic recovery of families.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/29f6szz

Keywords: COVID-19 pandemic, individual income tax
