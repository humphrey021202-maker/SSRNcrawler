Tail Properties of Distributions and Applications

11 Pages Posted: 5 Apr 2021

Christos Floros

Hellenic Mediterranean University, Department of Accounting and Finance

Konstantinos Gkillas

University of Patras

Christos Kountzakis

University of the Aegean - Department of Statistics and Actuarial - Financial Mathematics

Date Written: March 16, 2021

Abstract

In this paper we deduce some useful results about Tail Properties of Distributions and their applications in risk theory and risk management.

Keywords: Random Variables’ Transformations; Linear Regression ; Sample-ﬁtting; Tail Properties

JEL Classification: G22; C46

Suggested Citation:

Floros, Christos and Gkillas, Konstantinos and Kountzakis, Christos, Tail Properties of Distributions and Applications (March 16, 2021). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3805916 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3805916
