Risk-Neutral Monopolists are Variance-Averse

4 Pages Posted: 18 Jun 2009 Last revised: 22 Jun 2009

Roland Kirstein

University of Magdeburg - Economics of Business and Law

Date Written: April 16, 2009

Abstract

If the production of a risk-neutral monopolist is influenced by a random variable, then the expected profit is decreasing in the variance of the production process.

Keywords: risk-aversion, correlated random variables, market power

JEL Classification: D81, L12

Suggested Citation:

Kirstein, Prof. Dr. Roland, Risk-Neutral Monopolists are Variance-Averse (April 16, 2009). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1420739 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.1420739
