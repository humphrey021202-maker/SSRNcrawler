Duration and Convexity in Bonds Valuation (Duración y Convexidad en la Valoración de Bonos) (Spanish)

12 Pages Posted: 26 Apr 2010 Last revised: 13 May 2010

Jean Paul Loffredo

Independent

Date Written: April 25, 2010

Abstract

There is some kind of confusion about the use of Duration and Convexity and the calculation of them when we try to calculate a bond valuation. I’ve tried to bring some clarity to it.

Note: Downloadable document is in Spanish.

Keywords: Duration, Convexity, Bond, Fixed Income Valuation, Duración, Convexidad, Bonos, Valoración de Títulos de Renta Fija

JEL Classification: G12

Suggested Citation:
