Talent "Pipeline," Audit Quality, and Other Research Topics of Interest

19 Pages Posted: 26 Jun 2024

CEPR; University of Oxford - Finance; CESifo; European Corporate Governance Institute (ECGI)

Date Written: October 6, 2023

Abstract

How much of the apparent "shortage" of accountants could be due to persistently low wages due to monopsony power in the labor market?

Keywords: accounting, accountant labor market

JEL Classification: J42, L41, K21,
