Predictive Analytics for Accounting Fraud Detection: A Study Based on Integrating Corporate Governance and Underpinning Theories

Asian Journal of Economics, Business and Accounting, volume 25, issue 3, 2025[10.9734/ajeba/2025/v25i31699]

14 Pages Posted: 3 Mar 2025

Muhammed Sameer Uddin

Minnesota State University, Mankato; Universiti Tun Abdul Razak (UNIRAZAK)

Date Written: October 02, 2024

Abstract

Accounting fraud is a major problem in today's dynamic financial world, particularly for stock exchange listed companies in Bangladesh. Accounting fraud undermines investor faith in the market, affects financial stability, and deteriorates market integrity, posing a major threat to the Study Protocol

Keywords: Accounting fraud, predictive analytics, financial indicators, corporate governance
