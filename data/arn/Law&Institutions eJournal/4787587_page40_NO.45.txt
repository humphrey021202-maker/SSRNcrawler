What is the Scope of the Concept of 'Income' in Article 16 of the OECD Model?

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 71 (2017), no. 3/4 ; 16 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/2f65kr5

Posted: 15 Apr 2024

Andy Cools

Independent

Date Written: February 10, 2017

Abstract

The author examines the scope of article 16 of the OECD Model and specifically considers what items of income should be regarded as being directors' fees.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: OECD Model, directors' fees, force of attraction

JEL Classification: K33, K34
