Building on the Rubble of Pillar One

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 11/12, Pages 533-542

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Graeme S. Cooper

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author argues that Pillar One is crumbling before our eyes, but it might yet produce lasting, unexpected and welcome results if it establishes the group as one tax entity and brings to an end the sophistry of arm's length pricing.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/q2700q

Keywords: Pillar 1 (OECD), allocation of taxing rights, allocation of profits, nexus, MNE, transfer pricing, arm's length principle, developing countries

Suggested Citation:
