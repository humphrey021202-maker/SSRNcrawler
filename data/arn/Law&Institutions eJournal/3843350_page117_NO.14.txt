How Companies Can Adapt to the Green New Deal

Journal of Accounting, Ethics and Public Policy 22(1): 59-74 (2021)

16 Pages Posted: 13 May 2021

Sunita Rao

Washburn University

Date Written: February 8, 2021

Abstract

This article explores possible effects of the Green New Deal (GND) on corporate activities. To this end, the article examines the potential impact of the GND on US core management functions: financing activities, operating activities, and investing activities. It also explores the possible impacts of the GND on management information systems and on the accounting profession, auditing, and consulting.

Keywords: Green New Deal (GND); Impact of the Green New Deal on Management Activities; Impact of the Green New deal on the Accounting Profession

JEL Classification: F64, H83, K32, M40, M48, Q50, Q56, Q59
