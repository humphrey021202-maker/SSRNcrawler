Conceptual Problems of Beneficial Ownership and the Corporate Veil

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 3 ; 9 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/qe230w

Posted: 4 Jun 2024

Saurabh Jain

Independent

John Prebble KC

Victoria University of Wellington, Te Herenga Waka - Faculty of Law; Institut für Österreichisches und Internationales Steuerrecht, Wirtschaftsuniversität Wien; University of Notre Dame Australia - School of Law

Date Written: February 5, 2019

Abstract

This article examines how courts have confused the beneficial ownership test with the doctrine of lifting the corporate veil.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: beneficial ownership, economic substance, case law, agent, nominee, conduit company

JEL Classification: K33, K34

Suggested Citation:
