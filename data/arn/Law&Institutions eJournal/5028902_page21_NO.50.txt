Opinion Statement Fc 1/2023 on the European Commission Public Consultation on the Introduction of a New Corporate Taxation System in Europe (Befit)

European Taxation: Vol. 63 (2023), Issue 7, Pages 298-302

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

CFE Tax Advisers Europe

Independent

Abstract

In this Opinion Statement, submitted to the EU Institutions on 26 January 2023, CFE Tax Advisers Europe comments on the European Commission Public Consultation on the Introduction of a New Corporate Taxation System in Europe (BEFIT).

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1t31zj8

Keywords: BEFIT Proposal, corporate income tax, tax base, formulary apportionment, cooperative compliance, tax competition, tax certainty, dispute resolution, transfer pricing
