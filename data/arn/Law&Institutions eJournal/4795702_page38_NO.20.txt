The Taxation of Resource Companies in Australia

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 2 ; p. 99-105; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3fafckk

Posted: 26 Apr 2024

Anton Joseph

Independent

Date Written: January 16, 2018

Abstract

In this article, the author reviews the taxation of resource companies in Australia with an emphasis on both the past and on potential future developments in this area.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: natural resources, resource rent (tax), mining industry, deductions, capital gains tax

JEL Classification: K33, K34
