The OECD Global Minimum Tax Proposal Under Pillar Two: Will it Achieve the Desired Policy Objective?

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 76 (2022), Issue 1, Pages 44-52

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Pitambar Das

Independent

A. Rizzo

Independent

Abstract

The authors, in this article, examine the implications of the global minimum tax as proposed by the OECD under Pillar Two regarding the taxation of corporations, and whether the proposal will realize its stated policy aims.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3sn3mr0

Keywords: global minimum tax, Pillar 2 (OECD), GloBE (OECD), worldwide income, territoriality, tax base, IFRS, Pillar 1 (OECD), tax treaty

Suggested Citation:

Das, Pitambar and Rizzo, A., The OECD Global Minimum Tax Proposal Under Pillar Two: Will it Achieve the Desired Policy Objective?. Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 76 (2022), Issue 1, Pages 44-52, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5003188 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/3sn3mr0
