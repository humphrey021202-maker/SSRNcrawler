Taxpayers' Rights Before Administrative Courts

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 9, Pages 440-441

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Dagmara Dominik-Ogińska

Independent

Abstract

This article outlines some of the issues that taxpayers may face when appearing before tax courts in Poland.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/34wc3b5

Keywords: taxpayers' rights, procedural tax law, appeal
