Cfe Input on Extending EU Dac to Cryptocurrencies and E-Money

European Taxation: Vol. 61 (2021), Issue 5, Pages 218-221

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

CFE Tax Technology Committee

Independent

Abstract

This Opinion Statement, submitted to the EU Commission Platform for Tax Good Governance on 11 January 2021, provides the CFE's input on the questions of the EU Platform for Good Governance on Extending the EU Mutual Assistance Directive (2011/16) (DAC) to include cryptoassets and e-money.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3z423gx

Keywords: DAC, digital economy, cryptocurrency, exchange of information, blockchain, intermediary, reporting requirements, money laundering
