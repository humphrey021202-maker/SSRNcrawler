Restrictions on Interest Deductions and Tax Treaties

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 4 ; p. 170-185; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/306prah

Posted: 4 Jun 2024

Brian J. Arnold

Independent

Date Written: February 22, 2019

Abstract

In this article, the author discusses restrictions on tax deductions for interest in relation to tax treaties, with special reference to the new provisions introduced in the OECD Model (2017) and the UN Model (2017).

Full-text Paper

Keywords: OECD Model, interest deduction, tax treaty, UN Model

JEL Classification: K33, K34
