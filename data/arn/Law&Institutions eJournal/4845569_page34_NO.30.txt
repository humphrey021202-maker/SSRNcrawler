Complete Distributive Rules and the Single Tax Principle: A Review of Recent Italian Case Law

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 4 ; p. 186-197; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3d7kg6k

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

Reuven Avi-Yonah

Independent

Gianluca Mazzoni

Independent

Date Written: February 20, 2019

Abstract

The authors, in this article, examine the application of complete distributive rules as set out in various tax treaties as it relates to the single tax principle by reference to recent Italian case law.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: distributive rules, tax treaty, single tax principle, double non-taxation, residence, case law, ECJ case law

JEL Classification: K33, K34

Suggested Citation:
