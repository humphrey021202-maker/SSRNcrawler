The Impact of Governance Mechanisms on Financial Stability in Egyptian Banks

7 Pages Posted: 5 Oct 2023

Dr.Mohamed Khalifah

aculty of Commerce, Ain Shams University

Date Written: August 9, 2023

Abstract

The study aimed to study the impact of governance mechanisms on financial stability in Egyptian banks, as it included two main hypotheses that dealt with both internal governance mechanisms and external governance mechanisms and their impact on the financial stability of the bank, where the study population consisted of 350 questionnaires out of 400 questionnaires, 80 for each bank Of the five banks, they have been distributed and they hold the position of (general manager, administrative director, head of department) from (5) commercial banks listed in the Egyptian stock market, which are (Commercial International Bank - Egyptian Gulf Bank - National Bank of Kuwait "The National" - Bank National National Bank of Qatar - Suez Canal Bank), and (9) banks were excluded due to the nature of their activities (Islamic banks - export development - ...), where the recovery rate was (88%).

Keywords: Corporate governance, Ownership structure, Financial stability, Internal mechanisms, Incentive contracts, External mechanisms, Risk management, Governance framework, gement
