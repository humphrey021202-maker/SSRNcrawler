Debra: All that Glitters is Not Gold

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 76 (2022), Issue 11, Pages 521-531

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

David Ledure

Independent

P. Deré

Independent

M. Van der Velden

Independent

I. Bastiaens

Independent

Abstract

The authors describe and critically examine the European Commission's proposal for a common legal framework across the Member States of the European Union regarding an allowance system for equity financing, or "debt-equity bias reduction allowance" (DEBRA), and an additional interest limitation rule.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1p51yky

Keywords: DEBRA Proposal, allowance for corporate equity, equity capital, debt capital, financing, interest deduction limitation

Suggested Citation:
