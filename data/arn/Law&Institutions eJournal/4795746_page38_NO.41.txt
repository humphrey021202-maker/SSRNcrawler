An Analysis of 'Google Taxes' in the Context of Action 7 of the OECD/G20 Base Erosion and Profit Shifting Initiative

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 4a (Special issue) ; 21 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/14d0kx3

Posted: 25 Apr 2024

Yasin Uslu

Independent

Date Written: March 26, 2018

Abstract

In this article, the author considers the influence of "Google Taxes" in relation to Action 7 of the OECD Base Erosion and Profit Shifting (BEPS) initiative regarding the prevention of the artificial avoidance of the status of a permanent establishment (PE).

Full-text Paper

Keywords: digital economy, PE, BEPS Project (OECD), diverted profits tax

JEL Classification: K33, K34
