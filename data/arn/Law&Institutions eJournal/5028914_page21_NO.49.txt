General Report of the French Ifa Annual Conference, 16 November 2022 - Beneficial Ownership, Substance: The Search for Reality

European Taxation: Vol. 63 (2023), Issue 9, Pages 369-376

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Philippe Martin

Independent

Abstract

This General Report provides a summary of the presentations given during the annual French IFA conference, which was held in Paris on 16 November 2022. The topic discussed in this conference was ?Beneficial ownership, substance: the search for reality?.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2vzmc9z

Keywords: beneficial ownership, substance over form
