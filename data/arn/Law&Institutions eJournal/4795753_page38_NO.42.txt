Some Comments on the Attribution of Profits to the Digital Permanent Establishment

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 4a (Special issue) ; 3 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/1w0pzqm

Posted: 25 Apr 2024

Yariv Brauner

University of Florida Levin College of Law

Pasquale Pistone

International Bureau of Fiscal Documentation (IBFD); Vienna University of Economics and Business

Date Written: March 26, 2018

Abstract

In the authors' view, the concept of digital permanent establishment (PE) is the preferable solution to tackle the challenges of the digital economy. In this article, the authors comment on the attribution of profits to digital PEs.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: digital economy, PE, digital PE

JEL Classification: K33, K34
