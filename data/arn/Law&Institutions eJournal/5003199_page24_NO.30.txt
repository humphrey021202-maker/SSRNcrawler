Treaty Shopping in Canada after Alta Energy Luxembourg (2021): A Closed Door Without a Lock

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 76 (2022), Issue 3, Pages 110-122

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Michael N. Kandev

Independent

J.J. Lennard

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors consider the implications of the recent Supreme Court of Canada decision in Alta Energy Luxembourg (2021), which dealt with perceived treaty shopping in the context of the Canada-Luxembourg Income and Capital Tax Treaty (1999).

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/19tqq57

Keywords: tax treaty, case law, GAAR, treaty shopping, MLI

Suggested Citation:

Kandev, Michael N. and Lennard, J.J., Treaty Shopping in Canada after Alta Energy Luxembourg (2021): A Closed Door Without a Lock. Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 76 (2022), Issue 3, Pages 110-122, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5003199 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/19tqq57
