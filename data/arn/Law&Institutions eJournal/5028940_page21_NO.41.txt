Pillar Two Implementation: Bill Submitted to Parliament

European Taxation: Vol. 63 (2023), Issue 12, Pages 550-553

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Jan Neugebauer

Independent

S. Richard

Independent

Abstract

This note summarizes the Bill of Law Implementing Council Directive (EU) 2022/2523 on ensuring a global minimum level of taxation for multinational enterprise groups and large-scale domestic groups in the European Union, which was presented by the Luxembourg government on 4 August 2023, and amended on 13 November 2023. The authors examine the main features of the Bill and its amendments and focus on some key points.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2q30ha3

Keywords: Pillar 2 (OECD), global minimum tax, top-up tax, QDMTT (OECD), effective tax rate, safe harbour, UTPR (OECD), IIR (OECD), information return, penalties
