Norwegian Interest Limitation and the Eea Right of Establishment: Comments on the Efta Court's Decision in Pra Group Europe as (E-3/21)

European Taxation: Vol. 63 (2023), Issue 1, Pages 19-25

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Adam Zalasiński

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author analyses the EFTA Court decision in PRA Group Europe AS (E-3/21), wherein the Court held that a combination of an interest limitation and a group contribution regime resulted in a restriction on the European Economic Area right of establishment.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1n9efsq

Keywords: case law, fundamental freedoms, interest deduction limitation, EBITDA, group of companies, contribution, intra-group financing, discrimination, justification
