The Rights of the Taxpayer and Tax Litigation in Finland

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 9, Pages 418-419

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Vesa-Pekka Nuotio

Independent

M. Pikkujämsä

Independent

P. Saukko

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors examine some taxpayer issues that arise during tax procedures in Finland.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/8vh7vn

Keywords: taxpayers' rights, procedural tax law, domestic tax law
