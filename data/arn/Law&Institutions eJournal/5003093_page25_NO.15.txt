Inquisitorial Maxim and Jura Novit Curia are Taxpayers' Rights: For Sure?!

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 9, Pages 449-450

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Michael Beusch

Independent

R. Gani

Independent

Abstract

This article examines some of the problems that taxpayers may experience when they take disputes before the tax courts in Switzerland.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1gy8xv0

Keywords: taxpayers' rights, procedural tax law, appeal, burden of proof
