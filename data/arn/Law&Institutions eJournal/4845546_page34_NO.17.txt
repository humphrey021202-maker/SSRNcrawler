Pakistan's Efforts to Implement the Common Reporting Standard

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 2 ; 6 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3e26hyk

Posted: 4 Jun 2024

Hassan Bilal

University of Palermo

Date Written: December 10, 2018

Abstract

In this article, the author reviews Pakistan's efforts to implement the Common Reporting Standard so as to be able to counter tax evasion.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: CRS, tax evasion, reporting requirements, due diligence, automatic exchange of information, documentation requirements, income tax, CbCR, master file, local file

JEL Classification: K33, K34

Suggested Citation:
