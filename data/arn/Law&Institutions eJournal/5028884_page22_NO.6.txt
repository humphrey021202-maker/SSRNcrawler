Final Danish Decision on Beneficial Ownership with Respect to Dividends Following the ECJ Decision in the 'Danish Cases'

European Taxation: Vol. 63 (2023), Issue 5, Pages 206-208

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Bodil Tolstrup

Independent

N. Bjørnholm

Independent

Abstract

This note describes the Danish Supreme Court's final decision in the beneficial ownership cases with respect to dividends in response to the ECJ's 2019 decisions in Skatteministeriet v. T Danmark (Case C-116/16) and Skatteministeriet v. Y Denmark (Case C-117/16).

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/krp41y

Keywords: beneficial ownership, dividend withholding tax, case law, ECJ case law

Suggested Citation:

Tolstrup, Bodil and Bjørnholm, N., Final Danish Decision on Beneficial Ownership with Respect to Dividends Following the ECJ Decision in the 'Danish Cases'.
