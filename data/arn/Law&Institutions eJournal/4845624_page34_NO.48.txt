Why Can't the English ...?

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 6/7 (special issue) ; p. 365-368; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/28aep94

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

John Francis Avery Jones

Independent

Date Written: March 22, 2019

Abstract

The author should like to put forward the case that English-speaking countries should follow the lead of non-English speaking countries and, at least, make their tax treaties with a prevailing text in English, or possibly in English only.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: tax treaty, treaty interpretation, model tax treaty

JEL Classification: K33, K34
