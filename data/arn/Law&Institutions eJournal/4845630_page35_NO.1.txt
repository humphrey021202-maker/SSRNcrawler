President's Welcome

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 6/7 ; p. 299-303; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3g8sv5g

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

Murray Clayson

Independent

Date Written: May 17, 2019

Abstract

In this special issue of the Bulletin for International Taxation, the President of the International Fiscal Association welcomes participants to the 73rd IFA London Congress to take place in London from 8-12 September 2019 and provides a summary of the scientific and social programme of the congress.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: interest deduction, BEPS Project (OECD), investment fund

JEL Classification: K33, K34
