Transfer Pricing Regimes for Developing Countries

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 4/5 (Special issue) ; p. 316-324; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/31cwepg

Posted: 25 Apr 2024

Patricia Hofmann

Independent

Nadine Riedel

University of Muenster

Date Written: March 20, 2018

Abstract

The authors, in this article, discuss challenges in designing and administering transfer pricing regimes in less developed countries and outline administrative and policy options to address these issues.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: transfer pricing, developing countries, safe harbour, formulary apportionment

JEL Classification: K33, K34

Suggested Citation:
