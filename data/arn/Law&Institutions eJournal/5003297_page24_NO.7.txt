Report of the Proceedings of the Twelfth Assembly of the International Association of Tax Judges Held in Budapest, Hungary on 9 and 10 September 2022

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 77 (2023), Issue 6, Pages 238-259

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Bob Michel

Independent

Abstract

This report summarizes the proceedings of the twelfth assembly of the International Association of Tax Judges, which was held in Budapest, Hungary on 9 and 10 September 2022.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2wakkej

Keywords: case law, beneficial ownership, procedural tax law, tax treaty, VAT exemption, COVID-19 pandemic, customs duty, tariff
