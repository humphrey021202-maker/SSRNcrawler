Proposal for an EU Directive Laying Down Rules to Prevent the Misuse of Shell Entities for Tax Purposes (Unshell): Seven Steps Towards Disgrace or Redemption

European Taxation: Vol. 62 (2022), Issue 4, Pages 165-167

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Oana Popa

Independent

Abstract

This note describes measures proposed by the European Commission on 22 December 2021 to prevent the misuse of shell entities (i.e. the Unshell Directive proposal).

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3jnp69c

Keywords: Unshell Directive, shell company, substance requirements, exchange of information, DAC
