Quality Internal Audit and Comparability For Compliance to ISO 9001:2008

7 Pages Posted: 20 May 2016

Adrian Tamayo

University of Mindanao - Research and Publication Center

Date Written: May 17, 2016

Abstract

Organization members need to be responsible and consistent in implementing the Quality Management System (QMS). On this, internal audit sets primary importance to meet requirements of ISO certification.

Findings reveal that the internal audit proved to be effective in ensuring implementation of the quality system within the organization. Internal audit findings caused significant improvement in the implementation of the quality system. In addition, the audit quality of the internal audit proved to be effective and consistent with the external audit towards ISO 9001:2008 certification.

Keywords: ISO 9001:2008, academic institution, audit consistency
