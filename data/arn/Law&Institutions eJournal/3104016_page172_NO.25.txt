Selective Disclosure

15 Pages Posted: 25 Jan 2018

Richard M. Frankel

Washington University in Saint Louis - Olin Business School

Date Written: September 19, 2017

Abstract

This paper discusses the benefits and costs of selective disclosure. It argues that the "level playing field" metaphor presupposes that selective disclosure harms market participants.

Keywords: disclosure

JEL Classification: D00, G15

Suggested Citation:

Frankel, Richard M., Selective Disclosure (September 19, 2017). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3104016 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3104016
