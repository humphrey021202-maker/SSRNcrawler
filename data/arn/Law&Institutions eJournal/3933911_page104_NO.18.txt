Exploring Tax Transparency and Corporate Accountability

5 Pages Posted: 30 Nov 2021

Bunmi Ayo-Ogunmuko

Independent

Date Written: September 30, 2021

Abstract

This paper aims to explore the concept of tax transparency, its background, growth, and benefits to companies. Finally, the writer concludes this paper with suggestions on how organizations can transition to reporting on their global tax contribution worldwide.

Keywords: tax transparency, corporate disclosure, tax accountability, sustainable tax practices

Suggested Citation:

Ayo-Ogunmuko, Bunmi, Exploring Tax Transparency and Corporate Accountability (September 30, 2021). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3933911 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3933911
