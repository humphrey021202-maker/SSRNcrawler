35 Years of ECJ Direct Tax Case Law: An Historical Overview on the Occasion of the 60th Anniversary of European Taxation

European Taxation: Vol. 61 (2021), Issue 12, Pages 542-551

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Adam Zalasiński

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author examines the development of the ECJ's case law, focussing on the author's personal choice of the most important milestone decisions.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/29j1x89

Keywords: ECJ case law, direct tax, history of taxation, avoir fiscal, discrimination, residence, nationality, fundamental freedoms, tax treaty, MFN

Suggested Citation:
