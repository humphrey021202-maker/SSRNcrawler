Vat Consequences of Rewarding Staff with Vouchers

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 34 (2023), Issue 4, Pages 141-142

Posted: 26 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Gorka Echevarría Zubeldia

Independent

Abstract

In this Column, the author analyses the ECJ’s judgment in GE Aircraft Engine Services, in which the court decided on whether or not the issuance of vouchers under an employee recognition programme constitutes a deemed supply of services for VAT purposes.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/30626b5

Keywords: ECJ case law, VAT, voucher, supply (VAT), services

Suggested Citation:

Echevarría Zubeldia, Gorka, Vat Consequences of Rewarding Staff with Vouchers. International VAT Monitor: Vol. 34 (2023), Issue 4, Pages 141-142, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5034264 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/30626b5
