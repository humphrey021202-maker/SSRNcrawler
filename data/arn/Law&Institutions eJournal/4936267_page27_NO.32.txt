The Principle of Prohibition of Abuse in Eu Personal and Corporate Income Tax Law and Its Implications for the Abuse Tests in the ATAD

25 Pages Posted: 23 Sep 2024

Stefanie Geringer

University of Vienna

Date Written: August 25, 2024

Abstract

This paper includes a summary of the main argument of my German-language doctoral thesis (original title: ‘Der Grundsatz des Verbots missbräuchlicher Praktiken im Europäischen Ertragsteuerrecht und seine Implikationen für die Missbrauchstatbestände der Anti-BEPS-RL’). 

Keywords: ATAD, EU anti-abuse principle, GAAR, CFC rule, CJEU

Suggested Citation:

Geringer, Stefanie, The Principle of Prohibition of Abuse in Eu Personal and Corporate Income Tax Law and Its Implications for the Abuse Tests in the ATAD (August 25, 2024). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4936267 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.4936267
