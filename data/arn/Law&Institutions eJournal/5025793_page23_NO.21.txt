Dac6 Implementation in Sweden

European Taxation: Vol. 61 (2021), Issue 1, Pages 21-28

Posted: 19 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

David Kleist

Independent

Abstract

This article examines the implementation of DAC6 in Sweden, focussing on topics that are under discussion in connection with the law proposals relating to the introduction of reporting obligations.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3jjxp5h

Keywords: DAC6, reporting requirements, exchange of information, intermediary, legal professional privilege, penalties, cross-border arrangement

Suggested Citation:
