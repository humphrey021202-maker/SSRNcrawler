Access to Justice in the Tax Court of Canada

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 9, Pages 414-415

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Patrick Boyle

Independent

Abstract

The author, in this article, outlines how the tax courts in Canada incorporate access to justice considerations for taxpayers in the resolution of tax disputes, and identifies current issues in this area.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/eere7a

Keywords: taxpayers' rights, procedural tax law, domestic tax law

Suggested Citation:
