Reflections on the Founding of The Journal of Accounting and Economics

17 Pages Posted: 17 Jul 2024

Jerold L. Zimmerman

University of Rochester - Simon Business School

Date Written: July 11, 2024

Abstract

This paper offers some reflections on why and how Ross Watts and I founded the JAE in 1978 and some of the factors that contributed to its ultimate success.

Keywords: JAE

Suggested Citation:
