The Judicial System of Brazil: Legal Remedies Under the Brazilian Tax System

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 71 (2017), no. 6 (special issue) ; 8 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/34mt1cc

Posted: 11 Apr 2024

Bob Michel

Independent

Tatiana Falcão

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Thais de Barros Meira

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: May 29, 2017

Abstract

In this article, the authors provide an overview of the workings of the Brazilian judicial system, with special reference to the resolution of tax disputes.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: tax system, dispute resolution, procedural law

JEL Classification: K33, K34
