What Non-Us Investors Should Know About the New Us Tax Law

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 10 ; p. 614-622; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/tskkat

Posted: 29 May 2024

Huanyu Ouyang

Independent

James G.S. Yang

Independent

Date Written: August 16, 2018

Abstract

This article discusses the effect of the US Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017 (TCJA) on taxpayers with an emphasis on non-US investors. Specifically, it notes the reductions in various tax rates, the move from a worldwide tax system and to a territorial one and the introduction of anti-avoidance measures.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: foreign investment, TCJA, tax rate, pass-through entity, anti-avoidance, MNE, worldwide income, territoriality, BEAT

JEL Classification: K33, K34
