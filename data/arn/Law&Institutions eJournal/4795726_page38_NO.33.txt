Sales-Based Apportionment of Profits

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 4/5 (Special issue) ; p. 259-272; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/w23mjt

Posted: 25 Apr 2024

Ulrich Schreiber

Independent

Date Written: March 23, 2018

Abstract

The author, in this article, considers the sales-based apportionment of profits in the context of, and as a response to the issues raised by, the OECD Base Erosion and Profit Shifting (BEPS) Project.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: allocation of profits, BEPS Project (OECD), profit split method

JEL Classification: K33, K34
