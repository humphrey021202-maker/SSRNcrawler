Taxation of the Sharing Economy: Recurring Issues

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 4a (Special issue) ; 4 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/2qs2nh

Posted: 25 Apr 2024

Carrie Brandon Elliot

Independent

Date Written: March 26, 2018

Abstract

In this article, the author reviews the recurring issues surrounding direct and indirect taxation of the sharing economy, including obstacles to compliance and collection.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: digital economy, sharing economy, direct tax, indirect tax, tax compliance, collection of tax

JEL Classification: K33, K34

Suggested Citation:
