More Clarity Regarding the Deduction Limitation for Transaction Costs in the Netherlands?

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 4 ; p. 199-202; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3yz8rxk

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

Shie Yee Au Yeung

Independent

Lex Bekkers

Independent

Date Written: February 28, 2019

Abstract

This article considers a recent Dutch Hoge Raad (Supreme Court) ruling with regard to the tax deductibility or otherwise of transaction costs in respect of an acquisition or a divestment of a participation in an entity and the implications of the HR's decision for Dutch tax practice

Full-text Paper

Keywords: deductions, participation exemption, case law

JEL Classification: K33, K34
