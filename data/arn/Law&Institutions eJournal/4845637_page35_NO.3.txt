An Overview of the French Judicial System and the Rule of Stare Decisis

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 8 (special issue) ; p. 390-393; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/2xe4rna

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

Emmanuelle Cortot-Boucher

Independent

Date Written: July 23, 2019

Abstract

In this article, the author reviews the French judicial system, and its case law, with regard to how the system affects taxation, with emphasis on the use of precedents.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: procedural tax law, treaty interpretation, case law

JEL Classification: K33, K34
