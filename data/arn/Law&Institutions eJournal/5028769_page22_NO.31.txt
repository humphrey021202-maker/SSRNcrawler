Application of the Spanish Gaar to a Cross-Border Financing Structure Implemented by a Multinational Group Through a Netherlands Entity

European Taxation: Vol. 62 (2022), Issue 5, Pages 194-200

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Enrique Sánchez de Castro Martín-Luengo

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author discusses a case in which the Spanish general anti-avoidance rule (GAAR) was applied to a cross-border financing structure implemented by a US multinational group through the incorporation of a Netherlands financing entity.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1g88be0

Keywords: GAAR, case law, cross-border arrangement, debt capital, back-to-back loan, audit, beneficial ownership, substance requirements

Suggested Citation:
