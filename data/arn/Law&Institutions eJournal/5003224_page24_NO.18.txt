The EU Debra Directive: A Critical Appraisal from a German Corporate Income Tax Perspective of the European Commission's Proposal to Reduce Debt-Equity Bias Through an Equity Allowance and Interest Expense Limitation

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 76 (2022), Issue 10, Pages 454-463

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Benjamin Graßl

Independent

L. Engeln

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors consider the implications and the advantages and disadvantages of the EU DEBRA Directive, as proposed by the European Commission, together with suggesting remedies for the perceived drawbacks to the Commission�s proposal.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3w134t8

Keywords: DEBRA Proposal, corporate income tax, debt capital, thin capitalization, allowance for corporate equity, interest deduction
