Recent (2019) US Tax Treaty Development: Dispute Developments in Non-EU Jurisdictions, Treaties Awaiting Ratification and the Advance Pricing Agreement Programme

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 12 ; 3 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/1keks4e

Posted: 4 Jun 2024

Nana Ama Sarfo

Independent

Date Written: November 6, 2019

Abstract

In this article, the author surveys the cross-border tax dispute process and issues involving the US and non-EU jurisdictions. She discusses US efforts to include OECD standards within its tax treaties.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: tax treaty, dispute resolution, MAP, MLI, APA

JEL Classification: K33, K34
