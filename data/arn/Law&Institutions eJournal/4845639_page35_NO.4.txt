The Uniform Application of Tax Law in Hungary

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 8 (special issue) ; p. 394-401; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/2c0bs3j

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

Péter Darák

Independent

Date Written: July 9, 2019

Abstract

This article considers the principle of the uniform application of the law in Hungary, both in theory and focussing on issues involving administrative procedures and VAT refunds as practical examples.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: procedural tax law, case law, VAT refund

JEL Classification: K33, K34
