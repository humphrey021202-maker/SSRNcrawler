Analysing Recent EU Leading Transfer Pricing Cases

International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 30 (2023), Issue 3, Pages 126-132

Posted: 2 Dec 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Carmen Roth

Independent

Abstract

The reliance on article 107(1) of the TFEU to monitor the granting of transfer pricing rulings has been a controversial issue. In this regard, important jurisprudence has been rendered in 2021 and 2022. Has the Commission’s practice to use an autonomous EU arm’s length principle come to an end?

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1wrb59q

Keywords: transfer pricing, State aid, EU Treaty, APA, ECJ case law, arm's length principle, selectivity, comparability analysis, justification, proportionality, burden of proof
