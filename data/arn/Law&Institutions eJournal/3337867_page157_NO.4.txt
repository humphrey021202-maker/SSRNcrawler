Do Fiduciary Duties of Managers and Members of Limited Liability Companies Exist as with Majority Shareholders of Closely Held Corporations?

47 Pages Posted: 25 Feb 2019 Last revised: 15 Mar 2019

Thomas M. Madden

Marist College School of Management

Date Written: February 1, 2010

Abstract

5 state analysis of fiduciary duties of managers and members of limited liability companies finding their duties largely akin to majority shareholder fiduciary duties. Consider both statutory and common law.

Keywords: fiduciary, fiduciary duties, limited liability company, corporation, shareholders, members, managers

Suggested Citation:
