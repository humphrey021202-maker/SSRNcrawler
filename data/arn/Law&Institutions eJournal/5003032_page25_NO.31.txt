Articles 10 and 11 of the OECD Model and the Commentaries on the OECD Model (2017): When Clarifications Raise Further Doubts

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 1, Pages 32-42

Posted: 4 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Giuseppe Francesco Patti

Independent

Abstract

The changes made to the OECD Model and the related Commentaries throughout the years have generated doubts regarding the scope of articles 10 and 11. In particular, certain amendments to the 2017 OECD Commentary had the effect of raising uncertainties regarding the territorial scope of these provisions.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/smpey0

Keywords: OECD Model, OECD Model Commentaries, dividend, interest, beneficial ownership, conduit company
