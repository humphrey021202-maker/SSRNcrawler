Comparison of Proposals to Adjust the Permanent Establishment Concept to the Digital Economy

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 11 ; p. 581-589; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/zp818a

Posted: 5 Jun 2024

Karina Ponomareva

Independent

Date Written: October 3, 2019

Abstract

The aim of the article is to analyse the various proposals that have been made by various bodies (including the OECD and the European Union) and countries regarding the alternative formulations of the permanent establishment concept to establish how these formulations align or differ from each other.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: digital economy, PE, significant economic presence, allocation of taxing rights, allocation of profits

JEL Classification: K33, K34
