Effective Tax Rate Criteria and Source Taxation Under the Oecd's Pillar Two Proposals

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 77 (2023), Issue 9, Pages 386-393

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Luis Eduardo Schoueri

Independent

B.C. Fettermann Nogueira dos Santos

Independent

Abstract

This article aims to analyse how the effective tax rate criteria of the OECD's Inclusive Framework Pillar Two proposal affects source taxation, particularly with regard to its effects on developing countries as Source Countries.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/qqsxjs

Keywords: effective tax rate, Inclusive Framework (OECD), Pillar 2 (OECD), developing countries, source principle of taxation, withholding tax, UTPR (OECD), IIR (OECD), QDMTT (OECD), tax incentive, tax sparing credit

Suggested Citation:

Schoueri, Luis Eduardo and Santos, B.C. Fettermann Nogueira dos, Effective Tax Rate Criteria and Source Taxation Under the Oecd's Pillar Two Proposals. Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 77 (2023), Issue 9, Pages 386-393, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5003307 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/qqsxjs
