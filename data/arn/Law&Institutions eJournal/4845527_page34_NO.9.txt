The Multistate Tax Commission's Alternative to Litigation

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 12 ; 3 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/16w9dtd

Posted: 4 Jun 2024

Roxanne Bland

Independent

Date Written: November 6, 2019

Abstract

In this article, the author provides an overview of the Multistate Tax Commission's alternative dispute resolution programme, which assists states as well as taxpayers with managing the risks and costs associated with litigating state tax disputes.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: state tax, dispute resolution, alternative dispute resolution, arbitration

JEL Classification: K33, K34
