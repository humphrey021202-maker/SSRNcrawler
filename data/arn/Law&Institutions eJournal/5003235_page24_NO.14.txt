Spanish Supreme Court Clarifies Tax Characterization of Payments for Client and Operational Data

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 76 (2022), Issue 12, Pages 610-614

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Enrique Sánchez de Castro Martín-Luengo

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author addresses a recent judgment of the Spanish Supreme Court (Tribunal Supremo, TS), of 24 June 2022, in which the tax characterization of payments for the use of client and operational data is clarified for Spanish treaty purposes.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/162df9s

Keywords: case law, tax treaty, income classification, royalties, treaty interpretation, substance over form, withholding tax

Suggested Citation:
