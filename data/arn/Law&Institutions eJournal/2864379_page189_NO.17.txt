Advancing the Corporate Governance Research Agenda

Corporate Governance: An International Review, Vol. 24(3), p. 172-180, 2016

Northeastern U. D’Amore-McKim School of Business Research Paper No. 2864379

29 Pages Posted: 8 Nov 2016

Ruth V. Aguilera

Northeastern University (USA) - Department of International Business and Strategy

Chris Florackis

University of Liverpool (UK)

Hicheon Kim

Korea University Business School (KUBS)

Date Written: May 4, 2016

Abstract

This article is the incoming editorial to the special issue "Review of Corporate Governance", which was published in 2016 in "Corporate Governance: An International Review". The Guest Editors summarise the articles published in the special issue and introduce several new research directions and issues to consider.

Keywords: Corporate Governance

JEL Classification: G32
