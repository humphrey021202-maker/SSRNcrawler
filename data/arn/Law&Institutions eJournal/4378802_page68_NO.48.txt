Growing Relevance of Audit Committee and IDs - for Boosting Corporate Governance Framework

12 Pages Posted: 9 Mar 2023

Pammy Jaiswal

Vinod Kothari & Company

Date Written: March 5, 2023

Abstract

In this paper, the author has tried to explain the idea and intent of the law makers behind introducing the concept of the Audit Committee, its expected role and function in ensuring and boosting corporate governance given the terms of reference suggested under applicable laws in India with a brief global comparison.

Keywords: audit committee, corporate governance, independence

Suggested Citation:

Jaiswal, Pammy, Growing Relevance of Audit Committee and IDs - for Boosting Corporate Governance Framework (March 5, 2023). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4378802 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.4378802
