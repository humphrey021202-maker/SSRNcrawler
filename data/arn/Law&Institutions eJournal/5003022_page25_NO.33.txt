Taxation in Qatar: An Abadi (Non-Finite) Vehicle for Economic Diversification?

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 74 (2020), Issue 11, Pages 642-653

Posted: 4 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Maryam A. Al-Asmakh

Independent

Abstract

In December 2018, Qatar introduced a package of tax reforms that represents a major move towards diversifying government revenue and achieving its vision of becoming an advanced country by 2030. This article argues that taxation is the most effective way to accelerate Qatar's independence from gas revenues.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/b4qpef

Keywords: direct tax, tax reform, PE, corporate income tax, tax-free zone, tax haven, foreign investment, tax treaty, MLI, excise duty, customs duty, VAT
