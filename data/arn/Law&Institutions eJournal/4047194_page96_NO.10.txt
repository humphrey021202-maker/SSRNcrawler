Shareholder Primacy and Labor

19 Pages Posted: 28 Mar 2022 Last revised: 24 Jan 2023

Sanford M. Jacoby

University of California, Los Angeles

Date Written: January 17, 2023

Abstract

This essay reflects on the history of shareholder primacy and its current status in the advanced economies. It is a draft for symposium on Jacoby, "Labor in the Age of Finance," to appear in Comparative Labor Law & Policy Journal.

Keywords: corporate governance, finance and labor, unions, shareholder primacy, social responsibility, financialization, pension funds, payouts, buybacks, organized labor

JEL Classification: n20, n80, h75, b50 g3, j32, j00,

Suggested Citation:

Jacoby, Sanford M., Shareholder Primacy and Labor (January 17, 2023). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4047194 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.4047194
