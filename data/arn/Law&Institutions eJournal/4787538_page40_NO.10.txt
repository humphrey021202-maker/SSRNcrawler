Arbitration Under the OECD Multilateral Instrument: Reservations, Options and Choices

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 71 (2017), no. 10 (special issue) ; p. 568-591; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/gz63b4

Posted: 16 Apr 2024

H.M. Pit

Independent

Date Written: October 2, 2017

Abstract

Seventy states have signed the Multilateral Instrument (MLI). Part VI includes a mandatory and binding arbitration procedure for those issues that remain unresolved after two years under the mutual agreement procedure (MAP). This article considers the implications for the 26 states that to date have opted for Part VI.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: MLI, BEPS Project (OECD), arbitration, MAP

JEL Classification: K33, K34
