Taxpayers' Rights in Court: To Suspend or Not to Suspend

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 9, Pages 444-445

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Dennis M. Davis

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author examines some of the issues relating to taxpayer rights as well as the balance to be struck between the protection of these rights and the interests of the Revenue Authority that can and, in some cases, have arisen when taxpayers appear before the tax courts in South Africa.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1rhsk8s

Keywords: taxpayers' rights, procedural tax law, appeal

Suggested Citation:
