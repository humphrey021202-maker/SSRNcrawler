Guidance on the Application of the Principal Purpose Test in Tax Treaties

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 71 (2017), no. 3/4 ; 8 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3q5vea2

Posted: 11 Apr 2024 Last revised: 4 Nov 2024

Valentyn Kolosov

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: February 16, 2017

Abstract

This article considers the impact of the principal purpose test (PPT), which Action 6 of the OECD/G20 Base Erosion and Profit Shifting (BEPS) initiative has proposed should be included in tax treaties, the certainty it offers with regard to legitimate tax planning and various sources of guidance for its application.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: OECD Model Commentaries, tax treaty, principal purpose test, GAAR, BEPS Project

JEL Classification: K33, K34
