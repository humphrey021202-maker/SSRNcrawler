President's Welcome

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 6 (special issue) ; p. 341-344; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3yrzdkr

Posted: 23 Apr 2024

Murray Clayson

Independent

Date Written: April 18, 2018

Abstract

Introduction to the 72nd Congress by the president of the International Fiscal Association. The main subjects include (i) Seeking anti-avoidance measures of general nature and scope - GAAR and other rules, and (ii) Withholding tax in the era of BEPS, CIVs and digital economy.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: anti-avoidance, GAAR, withholding tax, BEPS, investment fund, digital economy

JEL Classification: K33, K34
