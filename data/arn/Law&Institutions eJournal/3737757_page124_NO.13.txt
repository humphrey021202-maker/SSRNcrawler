IFRS 17: Fixing a Moving Target

3 Pages Posted: 25 Feb 2021 Last revised: 20 Jul 2021

Michael Winkler

Shasat Consulting

Sunil Kansal

Shasat Consulting

Date Written: November 25, 2020

Abstract

The objective of this article is to highlight some of the positive as well as critical aspects of the recent amendments (June 2020 amendments) to IFRS 17.

Keywords: IFRS17, actuarial, IFRS, insurance

JEL Classification: G12, G13, G14, G15,G21, G24, G31,G32, G33, G34, G35, M41, P34,C5, C6

Suggested Citation:

Winkler, Michael and Kansal, Sunil, IFRS 17: Fixing a Moving Target (November 25, 2020). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3737757 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3737757
