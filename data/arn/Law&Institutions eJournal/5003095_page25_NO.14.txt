Taxpayers and Court Procedures in Spain

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 9, Pages 446

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Raúl C. Cancio Fernández

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author examines some of the issues that may arise when taxpayers appear before the tax courts of Spain and, in particular, whether their rights would be better guaranteed with an autonomous tax jurisdictional order independent of the contentious administrative division.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1mvrmjy

Keywords: taxpayers' rights, procedural tax law, appeal

Suggested Citation:
