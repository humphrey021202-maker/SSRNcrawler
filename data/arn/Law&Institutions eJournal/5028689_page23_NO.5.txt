Digital Services Taxes Across Europe in the Midst and Aftermath of the Covid-19 Pandemic: A Plausible Option to Raise Tax Revenue?

European Taxation: Vol. 61 (2021), Issue 9, Pages 403-410

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Mery Alvarado

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author provides a comprehensive analysis of digital services taxes across Europe with a view to determining whether or not they are an option to address the budgetary concerns of European countries in the context of the pandemic.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/129d41c

Keywords: DST, advertising tax, COVID-19 pandemic, tax revenues

Suggested Citation:
