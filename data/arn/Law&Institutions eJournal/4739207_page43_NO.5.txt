GAAP and Transparency: Assessing Microsoft's Approach to Reporting Interest Expenses by Consolidating Them with Other Income and Expenses in its Annual Report for 2023

4 Pages Posted: 28 Mar 2024

Haitham Nobanee

Abu Dhabi University; University of Oxford; University of Liverpool

Date Written: February 26, 2024

Abstract

This study delves into Microsoft's distinctive approach of amalgamating interest expense with other income and expenses in their 2023 annual report. While this practice may be justifiable under specific circumstances, this paper scrutinizes the pivotal considerations and potential repercussions for users of financial statements.

Keywords: Financial Reporting, Microsoft, Annual Report, Transparency, Interest Expense, GAAP, IFRS, Disclosure Practices

JEL Classification: M41, G34, G38, D82, M48
