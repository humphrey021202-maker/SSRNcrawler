Taxpayer's Access to and Support in Court

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 9, Pages 442-443

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Margarida C. Reis

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author considers some of the issues that taxpayers might encounter when they appear before Portuguese tax courts.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1e6pwgn

Keywords: taxpayers' rights, procedural tax law, appeal
