The Role of Precedents in Netherlands Tax Litigation

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 8 (special issue) ; p. 384-389; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/x582e7

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

Wouter Blokland

Independent

Coen Maas

Independent

Peter Wattel

Independent

Date Written: July 23, 2019

Abstract

This article examines the role of precedents in tax litigation in the Netherlands, with special reference to the case law and the statutory tasks of the Hoge Raad der Nederlanden (Supreme Court of the Netherlands).

Full-text Paper

Keywords: procedural tax law, tax treaty, case law, ECJ case law, MAP

JEL Classification: K33, K34
