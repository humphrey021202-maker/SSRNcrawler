Much Ado About Nothing? - a Critical Review of the TMW Decisions of the Supreme Court of Korea

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 4 ; p. 203-208; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/22x5k3f

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

Ji-Hyun Yoon

Independent

Date Written: February 13, 2019

Abstract

In this article, the author considers the taxation of funds that invest in Korean assets and derive Korean-source income with specific reference to the recent decisions of the Supreme Court of Korea in TMW (2015).

Full-text Paper

Keywords: investment fund, residence, limited partnership, entity classification, tax treaty, substance over form, treaty shopping, case law

JEL Classification: K33, K34

Suggested Citation:
