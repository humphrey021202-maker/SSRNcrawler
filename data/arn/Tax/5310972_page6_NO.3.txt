Goods and Services Tax in Singapore: A Reflection on 30 Years

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 35 (2024), Issue 4, Pages 153-155

Posted: 19 Jun 2025

Soo How Koh

Independent

Abstract

It has been 30 years since GST was introduced in Singapore. In this Column, the author explains the features of the system that are good in terms of its fairness, equity, administrative ease, efficiency and certainty, and where Singapore can do better.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/rwx7xe

Keywords: VAT, GST, indirect tax

Suggested Citation:
