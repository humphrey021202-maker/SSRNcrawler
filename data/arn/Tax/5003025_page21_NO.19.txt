The Virus in the Alp

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 74 (2020), Issue 12, Pages 702-720

Posted: 4 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Richard S. Collier

Independent

I.F. Dykes

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors consider various policy, technical and practical implications of the transfer pricing guidance on risk and capital in the light of the COVID-19 pandemic, with special reference to the OECD Transfer Pricing Guidelines and the arm's length principle.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/5ec69y

Keywords: COVID-19 pandemic, OECD Transfer Pricing Guidelines, arm's length principle, risk

Suggested Citation:
