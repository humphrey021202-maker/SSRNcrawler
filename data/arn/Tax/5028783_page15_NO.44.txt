Anti-Abuse Rules of General Application

European Taxation: Vol. 62 (2022), Issue 7, Pages 298-306

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Sholpan Issakova

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author examines Kazakhstan's anti-abuse rules in light of Action 6 of the OECD/G20 BEPS Project, which is aimed at preventing the granting of treaty benefits in inappropriate circumstances, as well as legislated taxation principles.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/f0x7p1

Keywords: anti-avoidance, BEPS Project (OECD), treaty benefits, good faith, abuse of rights, treaty interpretation, principal purpose test

Suggested Citation:
