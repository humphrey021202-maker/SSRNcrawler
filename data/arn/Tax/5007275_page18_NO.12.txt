Tax Rate Trouble: 15 Percent Is Too Low for Corporate America

184 Tax Notes Fed. 2095 (2024)

8 Pages Posted: 7 Nov 2024 Last revised: 14 Oct 2025

Doron Narotzki

University of Akron - The George W. Daverio School of Accountancy

Vered Narotzki

Independent

Date Written: September 09, 2024

Abstract

In this article, the authors argue that reducing the federal corporate tax rate to 15 percent would be a mistake that could exacerbate economic inequality and undermine international efforts to curb harmful corporate tax competition.

Note: Reprinted from Tax Notes Federal, September 9, 2024, p. 2095

Keywords: Federal Tax, Tax Policy, Corporate Tax

Suggested Citation:
