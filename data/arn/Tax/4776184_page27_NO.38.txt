Dutch CV-BV Structures: Starbucks-Style Tax Planning and State Aid Rules

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 70 (2016), no. 3 ; p. 173-181; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/34ng1ng

Posted: 5 Apr 2024

Jan Vleggeert

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: February 11, 2016

Abstract

This article comments on Dutch CV-BV structures and investigates whether the fact that the Netherlands does not tax earnings channelled into a CV may be considered unlawful State aid.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: State aid, advance tax ruling, tax planning, limited liability partnership, limited liability company, tax treaty, hybrid entity

JEL Classification: K33, K34
