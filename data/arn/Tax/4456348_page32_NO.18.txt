THE DEMOGRAPHICS OF TAX EVASION: Summaries of 5 Studies

38 Pages Posted: 28 Sep 2023

Robert W. McGee

Fayetteville State University - Department of Accounting

Date Written: May 23, 2023

Abstract

This paper summarizes the results of five studies that examined more than 20 demographic variables and their relationship to attitudes toward the acceptability of tax evasion.

Keywords: ethics, tax evasion, demographic variable, Malaysia, Switzerland, Netherlands, Poland, South Africa

JEL Classification: E26, H26, O17

Suggested Citation:

McGee, Robert W., THE DEMOGRAPHICS OF TAX EVASION: Summaries of 5 Studies (May 23, 2023). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4456348 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.4456348
