Reports of Pillar Two's Death Are Greatly Exaggerated

186 Tax Notes Federal 1471 (2025)

San Diego Legal Studies Paper 25-016

7 Pages Posted: 25 Mar 2025 Last revised: 30 Apr 2025

Adam Kern

University of San Diego School of Law

Date Written: March 10, 2025

Abstract

This article analyzes President Trump’s executive memorandum on the global tax deal. It argues that the memorandum will have a limited impact on the stability of Pillar Two. Because the United States maintains GILTI, the strategic dynamics that support Pillar Two remain largely intact.

Suggested Citation:
