VAT Treatment of Sales of Video Game Skins on the Secondary Market

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 36 (2025), Issue 2, Pages 61-65

Posted: 19 Jun 2025

Matthias Oldiges

Independent

Frédéric Salewski

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors discuss the VAT treatment of video game’s skins on the secondary market. Several working papers have already been published by the EU VAT Committee on this quite complex topic. In the authors’ opinion, their qualification as “electronically supplied services” is disputable.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/22r43yv



Keywords: VAT, cryptocurrency, e-gaming

Suggested Citation:

Oldiges, Matthias and Salewski, Frédéric, VAT Treatment of Sales of Video Game Skins on the Secondary Market. International VAT Monitor: Vol. 36 (2025), Issue 2, Pages 61-65, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5311014 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/22r43yv
