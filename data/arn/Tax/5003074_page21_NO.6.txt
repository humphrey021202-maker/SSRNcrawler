The OECD on Taxation and Philanthropy: An Australian Report Card

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 5, Pages 202-211

Posted: 4 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Ann O'Connell

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author considers the findings of the OECD report on Taxation and Philanthropy in general, with special reference to its application to charitable and related entities in Australia.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1zffg9p

Keywords: charities, non-profit organization, tax relief, income tax, VAT, property tax

Suggested Citation:

O'Connell, Ann, The OECD on Taxation and Philanthropy: An Australian Report Card. Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 5, Pages 202-211, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5003074 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/1zffg9p
