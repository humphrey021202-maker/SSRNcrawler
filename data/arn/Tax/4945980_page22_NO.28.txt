Prospects for Introducing Professional Income Tax in the Hellenic Republic

Public Economics: Fiscal Policies & Behavior Economic Agents Journal, 2024. ISSN: 0047-2727.

11 Pages Posted: 4 Oct 2024 Last revised: 15 Feb 2025

Valentin Chernyakov

Euroacademia

Valentin Chernyakov

Euroacademia

Date Written: August 25, 2024

Abstract

This scientific article examines the main aspects of the prospects for introducing a tax on professional income in the Hellenic Republic by analogy with other states, which could bring additional tax revenue to the state budget of the Hellenic Republic during the protracted economic crisis that has gripped the Greek economy since the 2000s.

Keywords: tax on professional income, self-employed citizens, professional activity, macroeconomics, tax system
