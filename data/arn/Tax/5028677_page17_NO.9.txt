EU Blacklist: Implementation Under Luxembourg Domestic Law

European Taxation: Vol. 61 (2021), Issue 7, Pages 313-318

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Ilaria Panzeri

Independent

Abstract

This article discusses implementation in Luxembourg of one of the defensive measures in the tax area provided by the Council conclusions on the EU list of non-cooperative jurisdictions adopted on 5 December 2017.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/11f6k7q

Keywords: blacklist, domestic tax law, anti-avoidance, beneficial ownership, interest deduction, tax treaty, CFC

Suggested Citation:
