Have the OECD Model and the Un Model Served Their Purpose? Are They Still Fit for Purpose?

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 11/12, Pages 588-593

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Stef van Weeghel

Independent

Abstract

In this contribution to the 75th anniversary issue of the Bulletin for International Taxation, the author considers whether the OECD Model and the UN Model are still fit for purpose. He also suggests ways in which the international tax system could be improved.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1ttjaya

Keywords: UN Model, OECD Model, tax treaty, MLI, Pillar 1 (OECD), Pillar 2 (OECD), investment treaty, tax policy, history of taxation

Suggested Citation:
