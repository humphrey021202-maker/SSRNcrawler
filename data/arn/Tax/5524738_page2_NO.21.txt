Right Taxpayers, Wrong Taxpayers, Deduction-Selling, and Proxy Taxation

45 Virginia Tax Review, No. 1, pp. 94-124

31 Pages Posted: 24 Sep 2025

Daniel Shaviro

New York University School of Law

Date Written: September 01, 2025

Abstract

This paper explores the issues posed by allowing actual or implicit "deduction selling" to be tax-effective.

JEL Classification: H20, H24, H25, H26

Suggested Citation:
