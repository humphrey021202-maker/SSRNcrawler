EU Vat in the Digital Age

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 34 (2023), Issue 1, Pages 5-6

Posted: 26 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Patrick Wille

Independent

Abstract

In this Column, the author focuses on one of the three objectives of the VAT in the Digital Age package that the European Commission adopted on 8 December 2022, which is modernizing the VAT reporting obligations.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3v3bwy6

Keywords: VAT, digital economy, reporting requirements
