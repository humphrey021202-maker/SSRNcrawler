Major Questions Raised by New US Excise Tax on Stock Buybacks

Finance and Capital Markets: Vol. 23 (2022), Issue 4

Posted: 18 Feb 2025

Heath Martin

Independent

Abstract

The United States has adopted a new 1% excise tax on the fair market value of stock repurchased by certain publicly traded corporations during a tax year. The statute has been drafted broadly, with the result that the tax may apply more widely than intended.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/wj6wdd

Keywords: share buy-back, excise duty, fair market value, foreign company
