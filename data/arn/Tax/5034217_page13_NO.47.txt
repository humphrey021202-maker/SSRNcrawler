The Duodecad Case: A New Function for the ECJ?

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 33 (2022), Issue 2, Pages 65-66

Posted: 26 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Michael van de Leur

Independent

Abstract

In this Column, the author discusses the Opinion of Advocate General Kokott in the ECJ’s DuoDecad case, which concerns the place of supply of services and the possibility of double taxation because of different qualifications.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1k5md6

Keywords: VAT, supply (VAT), ECJ case law, place of supply (VAT)

Suggested Citation:
