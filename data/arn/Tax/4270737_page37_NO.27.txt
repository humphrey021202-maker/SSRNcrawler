Undertaxed Profits and the Use-It-or-Lose-It Principle

Tax Notes International (Tax Analysts) v. 108 (2022)

6 Pages Posted: 23 Nov 2022

Allison Christians

McGill University - Faculty of Law

Tarcísio Diniz Magalhães

University of Antwerp Faculty of Law

Date Written: November 4, 2022

Abstract

In this installment of the Big Picture, Christians and Magalhães defend pillar 2’s undertaxed profits rule, arguing that it is supported by fundamental principles of international tax law and economic logic.

Keywords: global minimum tax; international law; undertaxed payments rule; undertaxed profits rule; multinational enterprises; OECD; Pillar 2; GloBE; income inclusion rule; legal principles; tax policy

JEL Classification: F23; F53; F55; H25; H26; H87; K33; K34

Suggested Citation:

Christians, Allison and Diniz Magalhães, Tarcísio, Undertaxed Profits and the Use-It-or-Lose-It Principle (November 4, 2022). Tax Notes International (Tax Analysts) v. 108 (2022), Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4270737
