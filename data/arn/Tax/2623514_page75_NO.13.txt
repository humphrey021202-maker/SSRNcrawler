Simulating Firm-Specific Corporate Marginal Tax Rates in a Canadian Context

Multinational Finance Journal, Vol. 11, No. 1/2, p. 77-96, 2007

20 Pages Posted: 26 Jun 2015

Amin Mawani

York University - Department of Accounting

Date Written: June 26, 2015

Abstract

This paper illustrates a methodology for estimating corporate marginal tax rates in the presence of tax losses, and within the context of Canadian tax law.

Keywords: taxation; marginal tax rates; tax losses; corporate tax rates

JEL Classification: G3, H25, M4

Suggested Citation:
