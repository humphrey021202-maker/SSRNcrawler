The Voluntary Carbon Market – Fast-Changing Landscape and Tax Implications

IBFD Finance and Capital Markets

Posted: 30 Dec 2023

Chris Winkelman

Independent

Pedro Guilherme Lindenberg Schoueri

Date Written: November 30, 2023

Abstract

As the voluntary carbon market gets more mature, market participants are faced with significant tax and regulatory challenges that can create uncertainty and may hamper the market’s development. This article provides an overview of the fast-changing landscape and illustrates a number of the policy, legal and tax questions that will be increasingly pressing in the years to come. Full text available at https://research.ibfd.org/#/doc?url=/document/dfi_2023_03_int_3
