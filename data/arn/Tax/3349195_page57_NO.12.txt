Municipal Bonds after the Deluge

"Municipal Bonds After the Deluge," 159 Tax Notes 521, January 22, 2018

14 Pages Posted: 1 Apr 2019

Adam Wallwork

Deloitte Tax LLP; Deloitte Tax LP; Deloitte Tax LP

Date Written: January 22, 2018

Abstract

Adam S. Wallwork is an attorney in the public finance and tax departments of Ballard Spahr LLP in Washington, D.C. In this article, Wallwork shows how tax reform has affected the municipal bond market and why Republicans should care.

Keywords: state and local governments, state and local bonds, bonds, tax-exempt, 501(c)(3), income tax, interest, financial instruments, debt, tax, tax policy, government bonds, governments, public finance, project finance, Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, tax reform, federal income tax, public policy

JEL Classification: H20, H23, H24, H70, H71, H72, H73, H74, H63, H50, H51, H52, H53, H54, H81, K00, K34, G18, G20, R50
