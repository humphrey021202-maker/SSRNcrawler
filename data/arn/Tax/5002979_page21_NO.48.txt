Value Creation: A Dimming Lodestar for International Taxation?

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 74 (2020), Issue 4/5, Pages 216-221

Posted: 4 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Rebecca M. Kysar

Independent

Abstract

The author, in this article, considers the possibly declining significance of the taxation of value creation in relation to international taxation, primarily as reflected in the OECD/G20 Base Erosion and Profit Shifting Project.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3093ffe

Keywords: value creation, BEPS Project (OECD), source of income, treaty interpretation

Suggested Citation:
