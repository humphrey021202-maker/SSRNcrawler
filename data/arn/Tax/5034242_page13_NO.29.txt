Back to the Future of Vat or Waiting for Godot?

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 33 (2022), Issue 6, Pages 229-230

Posted: 26 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Ine Lejeune

Independent

Abstract

In this column, the author questions whether the unpaid tax collectors would experience a high-quality “harmonized EU VAT system” if the Commission were able to adopt implementing measures approved by a qualified majority of Member States.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/32mq5sq

Keywords: VAT system, VAT Directive, ECJ case law, collection of tax

Suggested Citation:
