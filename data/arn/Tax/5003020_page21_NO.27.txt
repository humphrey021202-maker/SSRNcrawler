A Fresh Look at Article 3(2) of the OECD Model

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 74 (2020), Issue 11, Pages 654-659

Posted: 4 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

John Avery Jones

Independent

Abstract

The author, in this article, develops some new thoughts regarding treaty interpretation and the role of article 3(2) of the OECD Model from the common law point of view.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/xgw0x3

Keywords: OECD Model, treaty interpretation, qualification conflict, common law, domestic tax law, credit method, exemption method, source principle of taxation, residence principle of taxation, employment income

Suggested Citation:
