How Would Digital Tax Reform Affect the Cross-Border Marketplace?

Tax Notes International, May 4, 2020

6 Pages Posted: 31 Aug 2020

Yue Daisy Dai

Shanghai University of Finance and Economics - School of Law

Date Written: May 4, 2020

Abstract

This article discusses how the OECD and G-20 digital tax reforms would affect marketplaces, especially marketplaces with China-related inbound and outbound investments.

Keywords: digital tax reform, China's digital marketplaces, cross-border investment

JEL Classification: K34

Suggested Citation:

Dai, Yue, How Would Digital Tax Reform Affect the Cross-Border Marketplace? (May 4, 2020). Tax Notes International, May 4, 2020, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3627408
