The Brazilian Tax Implications of Cross-Border Remittances Arising from International Cost-Sharing Arrangements

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 69 (2015), no. 8 ; p. 450-62; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/1anw974

Posted: 29 Mar 2024

Leonardo Freitas de Moraes e Castro

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: July 13, 2015

Abstract

In this article, the author considers the international tax implications for the parties involved in international cost-sharing arrangements and, in particular, the arguments supporting the non-imposition of various Brazilian taxes with regard to payments made in relation to such agreements.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: cost sharing arrangement, withholding tax, local tax, expenses, deductions

JEL Classification: K33, K34
