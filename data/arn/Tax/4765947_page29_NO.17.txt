The Danish Regime for the Taxation of Passive Income for Groups of Companies

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 67 (2013), no. 4/5 ; p. 169-171; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/7rfcf0

Posted: 20 Mar 2024

Anne Becker-Christensen

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Nikolaj Bjørnholm

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: March 7, 2013

Abstract

This article presents a brief overview of the Danish taxation of passive income with regard to groups of companies, with special reference to the key features of the Danish controlled foreign company regime as it applies to such groups.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: passive income, group of companies, CFC

JEL Classification: K33, K34
