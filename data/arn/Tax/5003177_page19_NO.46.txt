Tax Competition's Terminological and Factitious Lure

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 11/12, Pages 571-578

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

John Prebble

Independent

Abstract

The author evaluates contentions that tax competition is a social good. He charts the progress of the OECD from apparently supporting to seemingly abandoning that stance.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/8wbvpe

Keywords: tax competition, harmful tax competition, tax policy

Suggested Citation:
