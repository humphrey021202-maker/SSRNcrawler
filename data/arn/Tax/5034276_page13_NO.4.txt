Vat and the Concept of Fixed Establishment: The Cabot Plastics Belgium Case

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 34 (2023), Issue 6, Pages 242-246

Posted: 26 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Karim Tourmous

Independent

A. Soldai

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors analyse an important ECJ decision in the Cabot case (Case C-232/22) concerning the interpretation of the concept of fixed establishment for VAT purposes, and explain the practical consequences that this case has for taxable persons carrying out cross-border activities.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1bzw22c

Keywords: VAT, fixed establishment, ECJ case law, services, cross-border services

Suggested Citation:
