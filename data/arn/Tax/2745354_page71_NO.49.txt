Interaction of Domestic General Anti Avoidance Rules with Tax Treaties

Posted: 11 Mar 2016

Vikram Chand

University of Lausanne

Date Written: February 28, 2016

Abstract

The author analyzes the interaction of domestic statutory general anti avoidance rules and domestic statutory specific anti avoidance rules as well as judicial doctrines with tax treaties.

Keywords: SAAR, GAAR, judicial doctrines, sham, business purposes, substance over form, step transaction, treaty abuse, treaty shopping, rule shopping

Suggested Citation:

Chand, Vikram, Interaction of Domestic General Anti Avoidance Rules with Tax Treaties (February 28, 2016). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2745354
