Hungary Introduces an Annual Transfer Pricing Data Reporting System

International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 30 (2023), Issue 3, Pages 154-158

Posted: 2 Dec 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Gabriella Erdős

Independent

Abstract

Hungary introduced detailed transfer pricing data reporting rules as a part of the annual corporate income tax return. It is aimed to give advance warning to the tax authorities about the potential tax planning structures and help them in the tax audit selection process. The new system substantially increases the compliance burden of the taxpayers.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/23xcc82

Keywords: transfer pricing, reporting requirements, audit, compliance burden, penalties
