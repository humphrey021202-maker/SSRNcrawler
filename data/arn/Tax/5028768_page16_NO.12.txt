A Comparative Analysis of the Amended Netherlands Tax Loss Relief Rules

European Taxation: Vol. 62 (2022), Issue 4, Pages 139-147

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Peter Kraak

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author analyses and compares the loss carry-over rules in the Netherlands, Germany, France and the United Kingdom.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2wnjm5c

Keywords: carry-over, loss relief, COVID-19 pandemic, domestic tax law
