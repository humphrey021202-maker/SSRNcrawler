Recent Developments in the Taxation of Indirect Share Transfers in South America: Lessons and Challenges from Chile, Colombia, Peru and Uruguay

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 9 ; p. 464-480; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/1jb7hp2

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

Gonzalo Suffiotti

Independent

Carolina Masihy

Independent

Date Written: August 20, 2019

Abstract

This article reviews the changes to the taxation of indirect share transfers instituted in some South American countries (namely Chile, Colombia, Peru and Uruguay) and assesses the significance of the new regimes.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: share transfer, tax treaty, case law, anti-avoidance

JEL Classification: K33, K34
