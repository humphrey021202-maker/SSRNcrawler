Hyosung Corp.: The Tax Treaty- Domestic Tax Law Interaction

Tax Notes International,Volume 118, No. 9

7 Pages Posted: 23 Jun 2025

Dhruv Janssen-Sanghavi

Maastricht University, Faculty of Law

Date Written: May 01, 2025

Abstract

In this article, Janssen-Sanghavi examines the question of how tax treaties interact with domestic law. He analyzes critically the recent Hyosung Corp.decision to illustrate the problems in interpreting the term “more beneficial” in section 90(2) of India’s Income Tax Act, 1961.

Suggested Citation:

Janssen-Sanghavi, Dhruv, Hyosung Corp.: The Tax Treaty- Domestic Tax Law Interaction (May 01, 2025). Tax Notes International,Volume 118, No. 9, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5297663 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.5297663
