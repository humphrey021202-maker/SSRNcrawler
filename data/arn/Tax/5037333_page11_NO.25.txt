Is ICAP a Viable Alternative to APAs?

International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 30 (2023), Issue 5, Pages 251-253

Posted: 2 Dec 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

J. Clark Armitage

Independent

H.D. Rosenbloom

Independent

A.N. Ghosh

Independent

Abstract

In this article the authors explain the OECD’s International Compliance Assurance Program (ICAP); why it came about; how it may compare to an advance pricing agreement (APA) process and whether it is a useful programme for taxpayers.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/w7bcxv

Keywords: transfer pricing, ICAP, APA

Suggested Citation:

Armitage, J. Clark and Rosenbloom, H.D. and Ghosh, A.N., Is ICAP a Viable Alternative to APAs?. International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 30 (2023), Issue 5, Pages 251-253, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5037333 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/w7bcxv
