Fiat State Aid Case Putting the EU Arm's Length Principle to Bed?

International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 30 (2023), Issue 3, Pages 111-118

Posted: 2 Dec 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Isabel Verlinden

Independent

S. De Baets

Independent

J. De Deurwaerder

Independent

V. Timmermans

Independent

Abstract

This article discusses the Fiat case next to the (potential) interplay and consequences thereof for the pending (informally called) Apple and Belgian Excess Profits cases.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/19ahhqm

Keywords: transfer pricing, arm's length principle, State aid, excess profits tax, advance tax ruling, APA, ECJ case law
