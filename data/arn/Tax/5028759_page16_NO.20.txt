Memorandum on Tax Treaty Policy 2020: Looking Ahead and Back at Netherlands Policy on Negotiating and Signing Tax Treaties

European Taxation: Vol. 62 (2022), Issue 2/3, Pages 51-66

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Frank P. G. Pötgens

Independent

P.I.M. Geerse

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors describe and comment on the government's Memorandum on Tax Treaty Policy 2020, presented to the Second Chamber of the Netherlands Parliament on 25 June 2020.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3d8jscp

Keywords: tax treaty, treaty policy, MLI, residence, directors' fees, entertainer, sportsperson, domestic tax law

Suggested Citation:

Pötgens, Frank P. G. and Geerse, P.I.M., Memorandum on Tax Treaty Policy 2020: Looking Ahead and Back at Netherlands Policy on Negotiating and Signing Tax Treaties.
