Transfer Pricing Adjustments and the Elimination of Double Taxation in Purely Domestic Situations

International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 30 (2023), Issue 1, Pages 35-37

Posted: 2 Dec 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Katerina Perrou

Independent

Abstract

This article presents the recently enacted amendments to transfer pricing legislation in Greece that provide for a mechanism for the elimination of double taxation that may occur after transfer pricing adjustments are effected in a purely domestic situation.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/7wx3t3

Keywords: transfer pricing, domestic tax law, audit, tax authorities, OECD Model, double taxation

Suggested Citation:
