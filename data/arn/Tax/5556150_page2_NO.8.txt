A Farewell Seminar in Honour of Wim Wijnen: Tax Treaties – What Are We Going to Do With Them?

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 78, Pages 131-135

Posted: 2 Oct 2025

Francesco De Lillo

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author reports on the Farewell Seminar, entitled ’Tax Treaties ’ What Are We Going to Do with Them?’ held under the auspices of the IBFD, Amsterdam, for Professor Wim Wijnen on the occasion of his retirement.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/23nnqs3

Keywords: tax treaty, MLI, STTR (OECD), GloBE (OECD), Pillar 2 (OECD)

Suggested Citation:

De Lillo, Francesco and De Lillo, Francesco, A Farewell Seminar in Honour of Wim Wijnen: Tax Treaties – What Are We Going to Do With Them?. Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 78, Pages 131-135, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5556150 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/23nnqs3
