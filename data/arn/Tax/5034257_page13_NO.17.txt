The Myopic Gst Update in the Maldives

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 34 (2023), Issue 3, Pages 131-134

Posted: 26 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Zaif Fazal

Independent

Abstract

The Maldivian parliament has recently amended the Goods and Services Tax Act 2011 to increase the country’s GST rates. In this article, the author discusses the change in the Act, the purpose of the reform and the impact of this reform on businesses and on the Maldivian economy.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/gw18t0

Keywords: VAT, DST, tax authorities, tax reform, reorganization
