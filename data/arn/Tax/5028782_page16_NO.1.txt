Taxation of Non-Residents in Kazakhstan

European Taxation: Vol. 62 (2022), Issue 7, Pages 313-315

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Almabek Zhabbarov

Independent

Abstract

This note outlines key provisions of the Tax Code regulating the taxation of non-residents, some practical issues that foreign investors should be aware of and recommendations on addressing these issues.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/tj7k82

Keywords: residence, non-resident, employment income, withholding tax, PE, treaty benefits, VAT
