The General Anti-Avoidance Rule of the EU Anti-Tax Avoidance Directive (ATAD GAAR): Interpretation and Implementation 

Working Paper of the Max Planck Institute for Tax Law and Public Finance No. 2024-08

52 Pages Posted: 18 Sep 2024

Aitor Navarro

Charles III University of Madrid

Date Written: September 18, 2024

Abstract

This contribution examines the general anti-avoidance rule of the Anti-Tax Avoidance Directive (ATAD GAAR) regarding the definition of its aim, reach and outcome (interpretation) and the way the Member States have implemented it into their national law, as well as issues of EU Law compatibility (implementation). It is forthcoming in the volume “Abuse of Law in European Taxation” (Springer), edited by Isabel Richelle, Wolfgang Schön, and Edoardo Traversa.

Keywords: European Union, corporate taxation, anti-tax avoidance directive, ATAD, GAAR, general anti-avoidance rule, tax abuse, tax avoidance

JEL Classification: H20, H87, F23, F63, K33, K34
