Spanish Supreme Court Limits the Use of Dynamic Interpretation of Tax Treaties

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 75 (2021), Issue 1, Pages 2-9

Posted: 4 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Enrique Sánchez de Castro Martín-Luengo

Independent

Abstract

In this case note, the author addresses two new judgments of the Spanish Tribunal Supremo (Supreme Court) of 3 March and 23 September 2020, in which limitations on the dynamic interpretation of model-based tax treaties were developed.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1d5671s

Keywords: case law, treaty interpretation, OECD Model, branch, PE, beneficial ownership

Suggested Citation:
