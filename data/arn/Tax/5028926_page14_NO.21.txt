An Overview of the Hot Proposal

European Taxation: Vol. 63 (2023), Issue 11, Pages 492-494

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Oana Popa

Independent

Abstract

This note describes measures proposed by the European Commission on 12 September 2023 to enable micro, small and medium-sized enterprises operating in the European Union through permanent establishments to interact only with the head office tax administration (i.e. the HOT proposal).

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2zjc3q8

Keywords: HOT Proposal, SME, PE, head office, tax simplification, tax authorities, compliance burden
