Today's Personal Income Tax: Measuring Change and Discovering Innovation

State Tax Notes, Vol. 78, No. 2, 2015

14 Pages Posted: 16 Nov 2015

Ralph B. Tower

Wake Forest University

Date Written: October 29, 2015

Abstract

This study expands on the author's 2005 research on the design of the personal income tax. The paper explores in depth the structural differences between the state and local tax regimes at December 31, 2014 and their 2003 counterparts.

Keywords: personal income taxes, state income tax rates, state income tax base

JEL Classification: H24, K34

Suggested Citation:

Tower, Ralph B., Today's Personal Income Tax: Measuring Change and Discovering Innovation (October 29, 2015). State Tax Notes, Vol. 78, No. 2, 2015, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2683580
