Voluntary Compliance

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 67 (2013), no. 11 ; p. 584-590; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/1qvc77t

Posted: 20 Mar 2024

Roman Seer

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: October 12, 2013

Abstract

In this article, the author describes the concept of voluntary tax compliance and discusses factors that have a significant influence on taxpayers' behaviour.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: tax compliance, enforcement, risk management, withholding tax, tax adviser, taxpayers' rights
