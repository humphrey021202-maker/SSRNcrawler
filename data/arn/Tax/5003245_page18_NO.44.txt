United Arab Emirates: Corporate Tax Relief on International Investment

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 77 (2023), Issue 3, Pages 94-112

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Howard R. Hull

Independent

Abstract

The UAE's new Corporate Tax Law introduces a competitive tax regime, granting tax relief to a number of business activities. The UAE also has an extensive treaty network, providing relief on international transactions. This article focuses on corporate tax and treaty relief on inbound, throughbound and outbound investment.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/14wpwxe

Keywords: tax system, tax relief, tax treaty, corporate income tax, foreign investment, MNE, global minimum tax, QDMTT (OECD), Pillar 2 (OECD)
