Abstract

This article presents the results of a comparative survey completed by tax practitioners throughout the European Union regarding how they evaluate and work with the new mandatory disclosure rules under DAC6 (2018/822).

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/5xe1qb

Keywords: mandatory disclosure, DAC6, reporting requirements
