VAT is Not a Barrier for Trade

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 36 (2025), Issue 2, Pages 49

Posted: 19 Jun 2025

Erwan Loquet

Independent

Abstract

After the inauguration of the 47th President of the United States, Donald Trump, there has been a misunderstanding on whether VAT is a barrier for international trade. In this column, the author analyses the topic and explains one of the fundamental principles of VAT: neutrality.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1z33zkf



Keywords: VAT, neutrality principle, CBAM
