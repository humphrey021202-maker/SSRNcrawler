Danish Implementation of Dac6

European Taxation: Vol. 61 (2021), Issue 7, Pages 291-296

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Bent Ramskov

Independent

M.B. Hansen

Independent

S. Ryan

Independent

J.N. Bech Balle

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors review Denmark's implementation of DAC6, focusing on four particular issues: the Danish method of implementation, the extent to which the rules laid down in Danish law go beyond the framework adopted in DAC6, the types of intermediaries subject to Danish reporting requirements and the imposition of penalties.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2te3m9a

Keywords: DAC6, reporting requirements, intermediary, penalties, harmonization of tax

Suggested Citation:

Ramskov, Bent and Hansen, M.B. and Ryan, S. and Bech Balle, J.N., Danish Implementation of Dac6. European Taxation: Vol. 61 (2021), Issue 7, Pages 291-296, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5028670 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/2te3m9a
