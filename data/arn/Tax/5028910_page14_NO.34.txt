Italian Tax Authorities Issue Public Ruling on Imported Hybrid Mismatches

European Taxation: Vol. 63 (2023), Issue 8, Pages 351-354

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Stefano Schiavello

Independent

R. Rossi

Independent

C. Ratti

Independent

Abstract

In this note, the authors comment on Italian Ruling No. 288/E, which clarifies the circumstances in which a hybrid mismatch should be deemed to exist as a result of the imported hybrid mismatch rule being triggered in a situation involving an Italian company that is part of a multinational enterprise group.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2esq8a5

Keywords: tax authorities, ruling, hybrid mismatch, MNE

Suggested Citation:
