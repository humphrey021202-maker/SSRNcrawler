What Will Be in Tax Reform?

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 71 (2017), no. 6a (special issue) ; p. 80-81; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/348w77z

Posted: 10 Apr 2024

Lee A. Sheppard

Independent

Date Written: June 26, 2017

Abstract

In this article, the author reports on the recent demise of the border adjustment tax and briefly describes the remaining elements of the proposed US tax reform.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: tax reform, corporate income tax, tax rate, territoriality, repatriation, interest deduction

JEL Classification: K33, K34

Suggested Citation:
