The Interplay between VAT and Customs: Liability and Deduction

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 35 (2024), Issue 3, Pages 120-131

Posted: 19 Jun 2025

Madeleine Merkx

Independent

Abstract

Dealing with VAT on import and export is complex, because of the interplay between VAT and customs legislation. In this article, the author discusses the liability for VAT and customs duties upon import and their interplay as well as the deduction of import VAT.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3khmf4j

Keywords: VAT, import, customs duty, tax liability, VAT deduction, ECJ case law

Suggested Citation:
