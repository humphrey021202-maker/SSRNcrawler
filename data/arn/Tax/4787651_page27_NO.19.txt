A Small Fish in a Big Pond: The International Tax Options of Belgium

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 71 (2017), no. 6a (special issue) ; p. 94-97; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/32zq5b6

Posted: 10 Apr 2024

Frans Vanistendael

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: June 26, 2017

Abstract

In this article, the author discusses the international tax options of Belgium in light of the one-page tax reform proposal released on 26 April 2017 by US President Donald Trump.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: tax reform, tax policy, BEPS, State aid, foreign tax credit, tax competition

JEL Classification: K33, K34
