Klaus Vogel Lecture 2022: Who Should Be in the Driver's Seat in Developing International Tax Norms - Civil Servants or National Parliaments?

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 77 (2023), Issue 4, Pages 157-163

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Bertil Wiman

Independent

Abstract

In his Klaus Vogel Lecture for 2022, Professor Bertil Wiman examines the question of "Who should be in the driver's seat in developing international tax norms - civil servants or national parliaments?", in so doing providing his answer to this important issue.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/21q3sr

Keywords: international taxation principles, allocation of taxing rights, tax avoidance, BEPS Project (OECD), tax treaty

Suggested Citation:
