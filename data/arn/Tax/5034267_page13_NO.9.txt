The Priorities and the Nice-to-Knows

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 34 (2023), Issue 4, Pages 148-151

Posted: 26 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Jochum Zutt

Independent

M. Timmermans

Independent

Abstract

In order to fight VAT fraud in the e-commerce sector, the Member States have decided to implement a monitoring system for cross-border payments originating from payers residing in the European Union. In this article, the authors discuss the new VAT record keeping obligations that will apply for payment service providers as of 1 January 2024.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2yhdv90

Keywords: VAT fraud, e-commerce, cross-border transaction, reporting requirements

Suggested Citation:

Zutt, Jochum and Timmermans, M., The Priorities and the Nice-to-Knows. International VAT Monitor: Vol. 34 (2023), Issue 4, Pages 148-151, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5034267 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/2yhdv90
