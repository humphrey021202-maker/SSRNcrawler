Enterprise Residence for Chinese Income Tax Purposes: Not What it Might Be Expected to Be?

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 68 (2014), no. 10 ; p. 541-547; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/14nq4vq

Posted: 29 Mar 2024

Nolan Sharkey

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: September 18, 2014

Abstract

In this article, the author examines the concept of corporate residence in Chinese income tax law and its interpretation by the Chinese tax authorities.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: residence, income tax law, case law, place of effective management

JEL Classification: K33, K34
