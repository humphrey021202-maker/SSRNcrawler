The Renewed Case for a Financial Transaction Tax

184 Tax Notes Fed. 2707 (2024)

7 Pages Posted: 7 Nov 2024 Last revised: 15 Oct 2025

Doron Narotzki

University of Akron - The George W. Daverio School of Accountancy

Date Written: September 30, 2024

Abstract

In this article, Narotzki traces the history of financial transaction taxes and examines several recent legislative proposals, arguing that imposing a financial transaction tax is good tax policy.

Note: Reprinted from Tax Notes Federal, September 30, 2024, p. 2707

Keywords: Federal Tax, Tax Policy, FTT, Financial Transaction Tax, Tax

Suggested Citation:

Narotzki, Doron, The Renewed Case for a Financial Transaction Tax (September 30, 2024). 184 Tax Notes Fed. 2707 (2024), Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5007287 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.5007287
