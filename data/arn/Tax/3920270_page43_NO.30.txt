International Taxation of Electronic Commerce

52 Tax L Rev 1 (1997)

U of Michigan Public Law Research Paper Forthcoming

50 Pages Posted: 21 Oct 2021

Reuven S. Avi-Yonah

University of Michigan Law School

Date Written: September 9, 1997

Abstract

This article submits proposals for taxing the digital economy on the basis of the benefits and single tax principles.

Keywords: digital economy, international tax, single tax principle, BEPS 2.0

JEL Classification: H24
