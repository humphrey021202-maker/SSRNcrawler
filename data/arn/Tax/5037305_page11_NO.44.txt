Digital Services Taxes: French Law Approach and Oecd's Commitments

International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 30 (2023), Issue 3, Pages 149-153

Posted: 2 Dec 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Pierre-Jean Douvier

Independent

C. Ankri-Avy

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors explain the tax treatment of digital services in French law, and the impacts of the new OECD standards harmonizing the taxation of digital services worldwide.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2jk3dyt

Keywords: DST, digital economy, domestic tax law, BEPS Project (OECD), Amount A (Pillar 1, OECD)

Suggested Citation:

Douvier, Pierre-Jean and Ankri-Avy, C., Digital Services Taxes: French Law Approach and Oecd's Commitments. International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 30 (2023), Issue 3, Pages 149-153, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5037305 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/2jk3dyt
