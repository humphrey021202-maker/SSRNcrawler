The Timely Resolution of Mutual Agreement Procedure Disputes: Secrets of Success?

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 77 (2023), Issue 5, Pages 213-228

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Michelle Markham

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the author considers the secrets of success regarding the timely resolution of mutual agreement procedure (MAP) disputes via an examination of key factors exhibited by countries that have received OECD awards for this specific achievement.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3mds71d

Keywords: MAP, tax treaty, dispute resolution, tax certainty

Suggested Citation:
