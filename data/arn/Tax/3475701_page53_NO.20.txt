Financial Transactions Tax – If Not Now, When?

165 Tax Notes 47 (2019)

6 Pages Posted: 5 Nov 2019 Last revised: 14 Oct 2025

Doron Narotzki

University of Akron - The George W. Daverio School of Accountancy

Date Written: October 7, 2019

Abstract

In this article, Narotzki discusses the short history of the financial transaction tax in the United States and why now is the perfect time to adopt it.


Keywords: FTT, Financial Transactions Tax, Tax Law, Tax Policy

JEL Classification: K34

Suggested Citation:

Narotzki, Doron, Financial Transactions Tax – If Not Now, When? (October 7, 2019). 165 Tax Notes 47 (2019), Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3475701
