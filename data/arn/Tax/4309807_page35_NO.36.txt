The Scope of Imposing Zakat and Income Tax in Saudi Law

Taibah University Journal for Law, Volume 1, Issue 2, December 2022،, 9-24.

Posted: 3 Jan 2023 Last revised: 4 Jan 2023

Ahmed Altawyan أحمد الطويان

Saudi Electronic University

Date Written: December 22, 2022

Abstract

This paper clarifies the scope of imposing zakat and income tax on Saudis and non-Saudis, as per Saudi law and the Saudi tax system.

Keywords: income tax, zakat, tax systems, Saudi Arabia
