Confirmed Fully Taxable Business (CFTB): A Step Towards Making UK VAT Fit For 2030?

5 Pages Posted: 29 Jan 2025

Fabian Barth

University of Lapland

Date Written: June 12, 2024

Abstract

This paper develops a proposal to simplify the collection of Value Added Tax (VAT) through the abolition thereof between fully taxable operators. For this purpose, it develops a draft bill introducing the concept of Confirmed Fully Taxable Businesses (CFTBs) and streamlining the rules on input tax deduction.

Keywords: UK, VAT, Reform, Value Added Tax, Consumption Tax, Sales Tax, Simplification, Improvement

Suggested Citation:
