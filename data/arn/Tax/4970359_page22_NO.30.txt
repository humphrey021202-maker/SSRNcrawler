Sourcing Derivatives: Time to Reverse the Rule?

6 Pages Posted: 3 Oct 2024

Reuven S. Avi-Yonah

University of Michigan Law School

Date Written: September 28, 2024

Abstract

This paper responds to a recent article on the application of withholding taxes on derivatives and questions the current state of U.S. rules.

Suggested Citation:

Avi-Yonah, Reuven S., Sourcing Derivatives: Time to Reverse the Rule? (September 28, 2024). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4970359 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.4970359
