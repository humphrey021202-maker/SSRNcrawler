Who's Left to Tax? Grappling with a Dwindling Shareholder Tax Base

18 Pages Posted: 17 Apr 2024

Steve Rosenthal

Urban-Brookings Tax Policy Center

Livia Mucciolo

The Urban Institute

Date Written: April 1, 2024

Abstract

Confirms and updates the extent of the shift to U.S. corporate stock ownership from taxable shareholders to foreign investors and domestic tax-exempt shareholders, and examines tax policy implications of that shift.

Keywords: corporate shareholder taxation

JEL Classification: H2, H21, H22, H25

Suggested Citation:

Rosenthal, Steve and Mucciolo, Livia, Who's Left to Tax? Grappling with a Dwindling Shareholder Tax Base (April 1, 2024). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4797771 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.4797771
