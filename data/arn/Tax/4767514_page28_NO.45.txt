Taxation of Services and Cross-Border Withholding Issues

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 68 (2014), no. 4/5 (special issue) ; p. 218-223; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/1j63srv

Posted: 25 Mar 2024

Sushil Lakhani

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Radhakishan Rawal

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: March 14, 2014

Abstract

In this article, the authors discuss the taxation of services and the related withholding tax obligations under Indian domestic tax law and tax treaties.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: services, cross-border services, withholding tax, domestic tax law, tax treaty

JEL Classification: K33, K34
