The "Biggest Tax Cut" from the Latin American Perspective

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 71 (2017), no. 6a (special issue) ; p. 89-93; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/2pe5pfv

Posted: 10 Apr 2024

A. Carolina Vaca Bohorquez

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: June 26, 2017

Abstract

In this article, the author examines the recently proposed US tax reform, which is intended to be the "biggest tax cut" in the history of the United States, from a Latin American perspective.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: tax reform, corporate income tax, territoriality, tax incentive, foreign investment

JEL Classification: K33, K34
