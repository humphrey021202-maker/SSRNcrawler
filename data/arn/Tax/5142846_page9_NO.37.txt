The United States Jumps on the Minimum Tax Train, But Seems to Be on its Own Track

Finance and Capital Markets: Vol. 23 (2022), Issue 4

Posted: 18 Feb 2025

P. D. Carman

Independent

Abstract

The United States has adopted a 15% corporate minimum tax based upon financial statement income. Rather than following the OECD GloBE template, the US tax follows a parallel course with similarities and differences.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/90w8ke

Keywords: minimum tax, alternative minimum tax, GloBE (OECD), carry-over, foreign tax credit, effective tax rate, top-up tax, GILTI
