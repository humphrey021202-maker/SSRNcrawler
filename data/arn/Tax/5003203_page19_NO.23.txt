Building a Belt and Road International Tax Dispute Prevention and Resolution Mechanism

Bulletin for International Taxation: Vol. 76 (2022), Issue 4, Pages 170-182

Posted: 6 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Xiaojing Cui

Independent

Jingxian Chen

Independent

Abstract

In this article, the authors consider developments since 2019 regarding the creation of Silk Road Economic Belt and the 21st-Century Maritime Silk Road (BRI) Tax Dispute Prevention and Resolution Mechanism (the �Mechanism�) under the auspices of China, as well as outlining how the Mechanism could be improved.
