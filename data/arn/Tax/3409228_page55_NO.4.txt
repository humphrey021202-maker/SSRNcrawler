The Tax Treatment of Liability Insurance Coverage

163 Tax Notes 1381 (2019)

FSU College of Law, Public Law Research Paper No. 910

4 Pages Posted: 16 Jul 2019

Jeffrey H. Kahn

Harry M. Walborsky Professor, Florida State University College of Law; Associate Dean, Business Program

Date Written: May 27, 2019

Abstract

This article reviews the policy justification for liability insurance tax treatment.
