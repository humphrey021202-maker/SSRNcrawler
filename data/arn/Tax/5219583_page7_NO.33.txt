Taxing Capital Gains at Death at a Rate Higher Than During Life

16 Pages Posted: 6 May 2025

Steve Rosenthal

Urban-Brookings Tax Policy Center

Robert McClelland

Urban-Brookings Tax Policy Center

Date Written: March 31, 2025

Abstract

Current law encourages people to avoid income taxes by holding their appreciated assets until death. We believe taxing gains at a higher rate at death than during life would turn the existing incentive for holding appreciated assets on its head, inducing more sales during life.
