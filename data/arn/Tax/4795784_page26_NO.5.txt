A Review of Comments on the Tax Challenges of the Digital Economy

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 4a (Special issue) ; 5 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/2dzz9sz

Posted: 25 Apr 2024

Barry Larking

Independent

Date Written: March 26, 2018

Abstract

In this article, the author examines the comments received by the OECD on action 1 of the base erosion and profit-shifting (BEPS) project regarding the tax challenges of the digital economy.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: digital economy, BEPS Project (OECD), withholding tax, equalization tax

JEL Classification: K33, K34
