Situs of Intangible Assets: Marketing Versus Trade Intangibles

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 68 (2014), no. 4/5 (special issue) ; p. 202-206; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/nj0wew

Posted: 25 Mar 2024

Sanjay Puri

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: March 12, 2014

Abstract

This article examines whether the situs of intangibles can be determined and discusses the tax treatment of transactions involving the transfer of such assets under domestic laws and tax treaties.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: situs rule, intangibles, marketing intangibles, capital gains, tax treaty, case law

JEL Classification: K33, K34

Suggested Citation:
