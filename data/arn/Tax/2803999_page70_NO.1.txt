The True Economic Effects of Corporate Inversions

151 Tax Notes 1819 (2016)

11 Pages Posted: 14 Jul 2016 Last revised: 13 Oct 2025

Doron Narotzki

University of Akron - The George W. Daverio School of Accountancy

Date Written: June 27, 2016

Abstract

In this report, Narotzki argues that corporate inversions are not as harmful as they are portrayed to be and that the government efforts may be better spent by allowing inversions or reforming the tax code to encourage businesses to remain domiciled in the United States.

Keywords: Tax, Corporate Inversions, MNCs, Multinational Corporations

JEL Classification: E62, H2, H20, H21, H25, H26, K34

Suggested Citation:

Narotzki, Doron, The True Economic Effects of Corporate Inversions (June 27, 2016). 151 Tax Notes 1819 (2016), Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2803999
