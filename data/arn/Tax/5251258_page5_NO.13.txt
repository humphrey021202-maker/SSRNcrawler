Taxation, Ethics & Public Policy: An Annotated Bibliography 1998-2024 

82 Pages Posted: 19 Jun 2025

Ashiqullah Pardisi

Independent

Date Written: May 09, 2025

Abstract

This annotated bibliography provides the abstracts, citations and links for all the articles that were published in the Journal of Accounting, Ethics and Public Policy between 1998-2024 on the topics of taxation and tax evasion, as well as the citations to hundreds of other articles on accounting, ethics and public policy that were published during the same period.

Keywords: Annotated Bibliography, Accounting, Ethics, Public Policy, Tax, Tax Evasion, Informal Economy, Demographic

Suggested Citation:

Pardisi, Ashiqullah, Taxation, Ethics & Public Policy: An Annotated Bibliography 1998-2024  (May 09, 2025). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5251258 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.5251258
