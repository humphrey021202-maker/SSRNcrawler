The New Portuguese Windfall Tax and its Potential Effects

European Taxation: Vol. 63 (2023), Issue 1, Pages 42-44

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Rita Botelho Moniz

Independent

Abstract

In this note, the author provides an overview of and comments on Portugal's new windfall tax on profits generated by companies operating in the fossil fuel industry.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/17dwgn6

Keywords: windfall profits tax, energy industry, mining industry

Suggested Citation:
