Preferential Local Tax Rates for Hotel and Lodging Taxes

Reprinted from Tax Notes State, June 24, 2024, p. 917

9 Pages Posted: 7 Oct 2024

Ted Peterson

University of North Texas

Date Written: June 24, 2024

Abstract

In this article, Peterson considers tourist models and conducts a review of literature pertaining to tourism taxes, advocating for the formulation of a policy changing the allocation of tax treatment favoring locals for lodging stays.

Keywords: tourism taxes, local preferential tax rates, lodging taxes, tourism tax policy, lodging tax allocation, hotel taxes, local tax allocation policy
