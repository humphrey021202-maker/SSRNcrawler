Tax Planning, Ethics and Our New World

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 2 ; p. 122-128; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3ap720y

Posted: 28 May 2024

Hans van den Hurk

Maastricht University

Date Written: December 14, 2017

Abstract

In this article, the author sets out his personal views on the current status of international taxation with regard to companies and individuals, and the ethical problems and dilemmas that this raises for tax specialists, both academic and professional.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: international tax planning, fiscal sociology

JEL Classification: K33, K34
