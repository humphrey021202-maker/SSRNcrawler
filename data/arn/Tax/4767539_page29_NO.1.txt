Intangibles and Location Benefits (Customer Base)

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 68 (2014), no. 6/7 (special issue); p. 352-360; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/336cfpc

Posted: 25 Mar 2024

Scott Wilkie

Blake, Cassels & Graydon LLP

Date Written: May 13, 2014

Abstract

In this article, the author analyses the issue of intangibles and location benefits, including the customer base, in relation to the OECD Base Erosion and Profit Shifting (BEPS) initiative.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: BEPS, tax avoidance, intangibles, location savings

JEL Classification: K33, K34
