The OECD Global Minimum Tax Proposal under Pillar 2: Will it achieve the desired policy goal?

76 Bulletin for International Taxation 1

17 Pages Posted: 15 Jan 2025

Pitambar Das

Central Board of Direct Taxes, Ministry of Finance, Govt of India; University of Oxford

Amedeo Rizzo

University of Oxford - Faculty of Law; Bocconi University; Stanford Law School

Date Written: December 15, 2021

Abstract

The authors, in this article, examine the implications of the global minimum tax as proposed by the OECD
under Pillar Two regarding the taxation of corporations, and whether the proposal will realize its stated
policy aims.

Keywords: Global minimum tax, International Tax, OECD, BEPS Action, Pillar 2

Suggested Citation:

Das, Pitambar and Rizzo, Amedeo, The OECD Global Minimum Tax Proposal under Pillar 2: Will it achieve the desired policy goal? (December 15, 2021). 76 Bulletin for International Taxation 1, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5040380 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.5040380
