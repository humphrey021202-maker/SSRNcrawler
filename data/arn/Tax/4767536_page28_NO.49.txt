Base Erosion and Profit Shifting and Interest Expenditure

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 68 (2014), no. 6/7 (special issue); p. 332-345; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3fqt3wd

Posted: 25 Mar 2024

Johanna Hey

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: May 21, 2014

Abstract

In this article, the author examines the implications for the utilization of interest expenditure in tax planning given the OECD Base Erosion and Profit Shifting (BEPS) initiative.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: BEPS, tax avoidance, interest deduction, EU law, fundamental freedoms, thin capitalization, withholding tax

JEL Classification: K33, K34
