Vat Registration Thresholds in Europe

International VAT Monitor: Vol. 33 (2022), Issue 6, Pages 286-289

Posted: 26 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Fabiola Annacondia

Independent

Abstract

As is traditional in the sixth issue of each year, in this issue of the International VAT Monitor an overview is presented of the VAT registration thresholds in most European (both EU and non-EU) countries, applicable in October 2022 and supplemented with announced amendments entering into force on 1 January 2023.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/3w6vdpn

Keywords: VAT, VAT registration
