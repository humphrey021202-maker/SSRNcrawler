Tax Cooperation between the EU Member States and Türkiye Regarding Automatic Exchange of Information on Income Collected by Digital Platform Operators

European Taxation: Vol. 63 (2023), Issue 11, Pages 506-509

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Leyla Ateş

Independent

Abstract

In this note, the author discusses the probable legal foundations of automatic exchange of information on income collected by digital platform operators between T�rkiye and the EU Member States. The note analyses the international legal framework, examines the extent to which T�rkiye has domestic reporting obligations for digital platform operators parallel to the EU reporting rules and provides some conclusions and suggestions on this cooperation.
