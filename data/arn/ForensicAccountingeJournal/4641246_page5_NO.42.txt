The future of financial fraud and forensic accounting

17 Pages Posted: 20 Dec 2023

Modupe James

Independent

Date Written: August 16, 2023

Abstract

As technology continues to advance at an unprecedented pace, so does the ever-evolving landscape of financial fraud. In this digital age, where transactions can be done with a mere swipe or click, criminals have found new ways to exploit vulnerabilities and deceive unsuspecting individuals and businesses. The future of financial fraud seems uncertain, but there is hope on the horizon in the form of forensic accounting.
This publication will delve into the depths of financial fraud, exploring its various types and perpetrators. We will uncover why some individuals are enticed by fraudulent activities and explore their motivations behind such deceitful acts. Furthermore, we’ll shed light on the severe consequences that financial fraud can have on both victims and society as a whole.
But fear not! There is a glimmering silver lining amidst this dark cloud — forensic accounting. We will discuss how utilizing cutting-edge technologies and investigative techniques can help prevent financial fraud from wreaking havoc in our lives. So fasten your seatbelts as we embark on an eye-opening journey into the captivating world of methods to combat financial fraud through investigation techniques infused with technological advancements for a secure future ahead!

Keywords: financial fraud, forensic accounting
