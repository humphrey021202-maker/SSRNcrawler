CONTINUOUS MONITORING AND ASSESSMENT MECHANISMS IN CYBERSECURITY: BEST PRACTICES FOR SUSTAINED PROTECTION OF CRITICAL ASSETS

12 Pages Posted: 13 Aug 2024

Mercy Ebute

New Mexico Department of Finance and Administration

Date Written: July 31, 2024

Abstract

This research explores the critical role of continuous monitoring and assessment mechanisms in cybersecurity, focusing on best practices for sustained protection of critical assets. In an era of rapidly evolving cyber threats, organizations across all sectors must adopt proactive cybersecurity measures to safeguard sensitive information and maintain operational integrity. This study examines the advantages of continuous monitoring, key assessment mechanisms, and the integration of these approaches to enhance overall security posture. Through analysis of realworld case studies and emerging trends, the research provides practical insights into implementing effective continuous monitoring strategies. The paper also addresses challenges in implementation and offers recommendations for overcoming these obstacles. By highlighting the importance of AI, machine learning, and predictive analytics in future cybersecurity efforts, this study aims to equip organizations with the knowledge to adapt and strengthen their defenses against dynamic cyber threats.
