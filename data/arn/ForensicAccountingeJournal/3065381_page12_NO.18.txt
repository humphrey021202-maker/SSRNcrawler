The Architecture of Fraud in the Accounting Environment

40 Pages Posted: 6 Nov 2017

Ahmed Riahi-Belkaoui

University of Illinois at Chicago - Department of Accounting

Date Written: November 4, 2017

Abstract

This paper introduces an architecture of fraud in accounting by explicating the nature of fraud in the accounting environment, providing some theoretical explanations of the phenomenon from the field of criminology, and exploring some outcome situations arising from corporate fraud.

Keywords: corporate fraud, criminology, outcome situations

JEL Classification: M40

Suggested Citation:

Riahi-Belkaoui, Ahmed, The Architecture of Fraud in the Accounting Environment (November 4, 2017). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3065381 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3065381
