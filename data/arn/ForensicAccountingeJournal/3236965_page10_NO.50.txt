Analyzing the Critical Effect of Creative Accounting Practice in the Corporate Sector of Ghana

38 Pages Posted: 31 Aug 2018

Haruna Ndebugri

Cape Coast Technical University

Emmanuel Tweneboah Senzu

Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) - Faculty of Law; University of Sierra Leone - Fourah Bay College

Date Written: September 29, 2017

Abstract

The studies strive to empirically examine, the adoption of creative accounting methods in financial reporting structures, in the corporate sector of Ghana and it ramification on the financial system and the entire economy as a whole.

Keywords: Creative Accounting, Corporate Sector, Financial Audit, Financial Reporting, Banking Sector, Investment

Suggested Citation:

Ndebugri, Haruna and Tweneboah Senzu, Emmanuel, Analyzing the Critical Effect of Creative Accounting Practice in the Corporate Sector of Ghana (September 29, 2017). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3236965 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3236965
