Optimal Accounting Policies Under Financial Constraints: Aggressive Versus Conservative

13 Pages Posted: 10 Mar 2011

Masatomo Akita

The University of Osaka

Yusuke Osaki

Waseda University - School of Commerce

Date Written: March 10, 2011

Abstract

We examine how severity of financial constraints influences a firm’s choice of accounting policy. This paper shows that firms with mild financial constraints choose an aggressive accounting policy and those with severe financial constraints choose a conservative accounting policy.

JEL Classification: D82, M41

Suggested Citation:

Akita, Masatomo and Osaki, Yusuke, Optimal Accounting Policies Under Financial Constraints: Aggressive Versus Conservative (March 10, 2011). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1782602 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.1782602
