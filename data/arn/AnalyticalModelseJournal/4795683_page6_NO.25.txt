(Re)Defining the Balance between Tax Transparency and Tax Privacy in Big Data Analytics

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 11 ; p. 656-663; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/1za3y9w

Posted: 28 May 2024

Gianluca Mazzoni

University of Michigan at Ann Arbor - Law School

Date Written: October 24, 2018

Abstract

This article considers the implications of the collection and the use of Big Data as it may affect tax transparency, tax authorities and the rights of taxpayers.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: automatic exchange of information, automation, big data, data analytics, audit, e-commerce, electronic filing, internet, tax transparency, tax authorities, taxpayers' rights

JEL Classification: K33, K34
