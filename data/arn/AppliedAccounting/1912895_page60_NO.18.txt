Did You Forget to Rollover Your IRA within 60 Days? Maybe You Were Depressed and Should Ask for a Waiver

7 Pages Posted: 21 Aug 2011 Last revised: 13 Sep 2011

Nicholas A. Paleveda

Northeastern University (USA)

Date Written: August 19, 2011

Abstract

You must roll qualified plan assets to an IRA within 60 days, but what if you are depressed and miss the deadline?

Keywords: IRA, 60 Day Rule, Rollover

JEL Classification: K34

Suggested Citation:
