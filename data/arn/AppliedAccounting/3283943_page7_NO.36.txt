The Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017 (TCJA) and Its Impact on Investors, Their Trusts, Investment Entities, Retirement Plans and Estates -- Part 2 Business Tax Reform and Part 3 Compensation Reforms

Journal of Taxation of Financial Products, Vol. 16, No. 1, 2018

26 Pages Posted: 6 Dec 2018

Dean Marsan

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: August 30, 2018

Abstract

This article continues Part 1 (see SSRN submission) and examines the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017 (TCJA) and its impact on investors, their trusts, investment entities, retirement plans and estates. Part 2 analyzes the business tax reform and Part 3, the compensation reforms.

Keywords: Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017, TCJA, business and compensation tax reform, corporate tax rate, sunset, capital expenditures, alternaive minimum tax, interest expense, NOL, research ,credit, real proprerty, RSU, equity grants, deferred compensation, fringe benefits, family and medical leave,

JEL Classification: K34

Suggested Citation:
