Taxpayers and Cheque Refunds: Sanity Prevails – Stabilpave (Pty) Limited v South African Revenue Services

Journal of Contemporary Roman-Dutch Law, Vol. 77, p. 346-349, 2014

4 Pages Posted: 6 Jan 2015

C. J. Nagel

University of Pretoria - Faculty of Law

JT Pretorius

University of South Africa (UNISA) - School of Law

Date Written: May 5, 2014

Abstract

The Stabilpave case brings clarity to the question when and under what circumstances the risk of theft or loss of a cheque in the mail passes to the creditor.

Keywords: cheque, mail, loss of cheque, risk

Suggested Citation:

Nagel, C. J. and Pretorius, Joseph Thomas, Taxpayers and Cheque Refunds: Sanity Prevails – Stabilpave (Pty) Limited v South African Revenue Services (May 5, 2014). Journal of Contemporary Roman-Dutch Law, Vol. 77, p. 346-349, 2014, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2545260
