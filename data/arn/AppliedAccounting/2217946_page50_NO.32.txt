Statutory and Effective Tax Rates: Part 1

Tax Notes, Vol. 136, No. 8, 2012

5 Pages Posted: 14 Feb 2013

Alan D. Viard

American Enterprise Institute

Date Written: August 20, 2012

Abstract

In the first article of a two-part series, Viard argues that a tax system’s work disincentives depend not only on statutory wage tax rates but also on the tax treatment of the consumption and saving for which wages are used.

Suggested Citation:

Viard, Alan D., Statutory and Effective Tax Rates: Part 1 (August 20, 2012). Tax Notes, Vol. 136, No. 8, 2012, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2217946
