A Case Study on Detecting Error-Structures in Costing Systems

6 Pages Posted: 19 Oct 2017

Rainer Lueg

Leuphana University of Lueneburg - Department of Accounting, Business & Management; University of Southern Denmark - Department of Accounting, Business & Management

Henrik Morratz

Aarhus University

Date Written: January 1, 2017

Abstract

This paper proposes a framework to identify when possible errors occur in the design and implementation stages of costing systems, specifically time-driven activity-based costing.

Keywords: activity-based costing, error-structure, time equation

JEL Classification: M00, M10, M21, M41

Suggested Citation:

Lueg, Rainer and Morratz, Henrik, A Case Study on Detecting Error-Structures in Costing Systems (January 1, 2017). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3055671 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3055671
