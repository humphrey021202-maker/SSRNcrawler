The IRS's Tax Determination Authority

71 Tax Law. 143 (2017)

36 Pages Posted: 11 Jan 2018

Allen Madison

University of South Dakota Law School

Date Written: December 31, 2017

Abstract

This paper discusses the IRS's procedures for reviewing and examining tax returns, negotiating tax liabilities after an audit, and the IRS's final determination subject to judicial review.

Keywords: administrative review, audit, examination, privilege, appeal, negotiation, determination, deficiency notice, notice of disallowance, refund claim

JEL Classification: K00, K01, K34, M48, H00, H1, H10, H11, H19, H2, H20, H22, H24, H26, H29, H83, K4, K40, K41, K42, K49

Suggested Citation:
