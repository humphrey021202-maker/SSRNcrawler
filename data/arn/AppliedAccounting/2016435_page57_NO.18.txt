Giving Useful Tax Planning Advice

Tax Notes, Vol. 134, No. 10, p. 1299, 2012

7 Pages Posted: 6 Mar 2012

Heather M. Field

UC Law, San Francisco

Date Written: March 5, 2012

Abstract

In an effort to ease the transition from school to practice, Field provides guidance to early-career tax lawyers about how to turn their growing knowledge of the tax law into useful client advice.

Keywords: taxation, tax planning, education, client advising

Suggested Citation:
