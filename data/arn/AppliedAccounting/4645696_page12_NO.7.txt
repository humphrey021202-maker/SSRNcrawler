Clawback Policies for Public Companies: What’s New and Next Steps to Conform to New Listing Standards

Journal of Compensation and Benefits May/June 2023

6 Pages Posted: 18 Jan 2024

James Reda

Arthur J. Gallagher & Co. Human Resources Consulting - New York Office

Date Written: May/June 2023

Abstract

This article details the SEC's new clawback provisions (adopted on October 26, 2022) that may affect various agreements, and offers guidance and action items for conforming to these new rules.

Keywords: clawback, clawback provisions, SEC, executive compensation

Suggested Citation:
