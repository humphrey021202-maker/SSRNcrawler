Tax Reform on Homeownership and the Impact on Children

Tax Notes, Vol. 101, No. 195, pp. 211-213, 2018

3 Pages Posted: 27 Dec 2018

Ryan Shouse

Indiana University, Robert H. McKinney School of Law, Students

Margaret Ryznar

Independent

Date Written: 2018

Abstract

This article discusses several homeownership provisions modified by the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, and how they affect children.

Keywords: tax, tax policy, homeownership, family

Suggested Citation:

Shouse, Ryan and Ryznar, Margaret, Tax Reform on Homeownership and the Impact on Children (2018). Tax Notes, Vol. 101, No. 195, pp. 211-213, 2018, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3282098
