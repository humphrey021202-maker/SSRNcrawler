The Pipeline, Graduate Accountancy Programs, and the 150-Hour Rule

181 Tax Notes Federal 1051 (Nov. 6, 2023).

17 Pages Posted: 20 Dec 2023

Mark J. Cowan

Boise State University -Department of Accountancy

Joshua Filzen

Boise State University

Troy Hyatt

Boise State University - Department of Accountancy

Date Written: November 6, 2023

Abstract

This article explains how diluting or eliminating the requirement that candidates for CPA licensure earn 150 academic credit hours could have unintended negative effects on regional graduate accountancy programs, their students, and the accounting profession.

Keywords: CPA Pipeline, 150-Hour Rule, Graduate Accountancy Programs

JEL Classification: M40, M41, M42, M48

Suggested Citation:

Cowan, Mark J. and Filzen, Joshua and Hyatt, Troy, The Pipeline, Graduate Accountancy Programs, and the 150-Hour Rule (November 6, 2023). 181 Tax Notes Federal 1051 (Nov. 6, 2023). , Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4653077
