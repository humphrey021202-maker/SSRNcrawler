Tax Implications of Contributing Appreciated Property Overseas

179 Tax Notes Fed. 431 (2023)

6 Pages Posted: 18 May 2023 Last revised: 14 Oct 2025

Doron Narotzki

University of Akron - The George W. Daverio School of Accountancy

Tamir Shanan

College of Management

Date Written: April 17, 2023

Abstract

In this article, Narotzki and Shanan examine the application of section 367 to section 351 exchanges and the income inclusion issues that may arise upon a U.S. person’s contribution of property to a foreign entity.

Keywords: Tax, International Tax, Corporate Tax, Section 351, Section 367

JEL Classification: K34

Suggested Citation:

Narotzki, Doron and Shanan, Tamir, Tax Implications of Contributing Appreciated Property Overseas (April 17, 2023). 179 Tax Notes Fed. 431 (2023), Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4448695
