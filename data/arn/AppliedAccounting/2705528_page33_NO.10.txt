For Corporate Taxes: Lower Rates, Raise Revenues

Tax Notes, Vol. 132, No. 745, 2011

5 Pages Posted: 20 Dec 2015

Bill Parks

University of Idaho - College of Business & Economics

Date Written: August 15, 2011

Abstract

This article presents a plan to streamline corporate taxes that would raise revenue while ensuring fairness and lowering statutory rates.

Keywords: Corporate Tax, Sales Factor Apportionment, Destination-based Corporate tax, Single Sales Factor, Interest deduction, Graduated Corporate tax, IFRS, GAAP, Combined Reporting, Interest Deduction, Depreciation

JEL Classification: H2, H21, H25, H26, H6, G38, K34

Suggested Citation:
