The Trust Fund Recovery Penalty

The CPA Journal, November 2017 Edition

2 Pages Posted: 11 May 2020

Kevin Flynn

Kostelanetz & Fink, LLP

Date Written: November 1, 2017

Abstract

Under Internal Revenue Code (IRC) section 6672(a), an individual can be held personally liable for a penalty for the willful failure to collect, account for, and pay to the IRS the employment taxes of a business.

Suggested Citation:

Flynn, Kevin, The Trust Fund Recovery Penalty (November 1, 2017). The CPA Journal, November 2017 Edition, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3578012
