Cross-­Border Restructuring and 'Final Losses'

European Taxation, 2012 (Volume 52), No. 10

11 Pages Posted: 21 Nov 2014

Frederik Boulogne

VU University Amsterdam; VU University Amsterdam - Faculty of Law

Nana Sumrada

Socius Inc. Ljubljana

Date Written: September 3, 2012

Abstract

In this article, the authors discuss loss compensation in connection with cross-­border reorganizations, in particular the extent to which the “no possibilities” test can be relied on in claiming compensation for “final losses”.

Keywords: Cross-Border Restructuring, Final Losses

Suggested Citation:

Boulogne, Frederik and Boulogne, Frederik and Sumrada, Nana, Cross-­Border Restructuring and 'Final Losses' (September 3, 2012). European Taxation, 2012 (Volume 52), No. 10, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2527886
