Woods: A Path Through the Penalty Maze

Tax Notes, Vol. 142, 2014

10 Pages Posted: 26 Feb 2014

Karen C. Burke

University of Florida Levin College of Law

Grayson M.P. McCouch

University of Florida Levin College of Law

Date Written: February 24, 2014

Abstract

This article discusses the Supreme Court’s Woods decision concerning partnership-level penalty jurisdiction in TEFRA proceedings, and explores the implications of the decision for subsequent partner-level proceedings.

Keywords: Woods, tax shelter, penalties, partnership

JEL Classification: K34

Suggested Citation:

Burke, Karen C. and McCouch, Grayson M.P., Woods: A Path Through the Penalty Maze (February 24, 2014). Tax Notes, Vol. 142, 2014, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2400503
