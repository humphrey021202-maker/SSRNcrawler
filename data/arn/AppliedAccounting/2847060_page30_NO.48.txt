Recovery of Overpaid Tax Under English Law

20 Pages Posted: 4 Oct 2016

Charles Mitchell

University College London - Faculty of Laws

Date Written: October 1, 2016

Abstract

The paper discusses the impact on the English law of unjust enrichment of a series of class actions in the 2000s and 2010s to recover money paid as tax under sections of the UK tax legislation that were found to have been contrary to EU law.

Keywords: unjust enrichment, restitution, enrichment, compound interest, public/private law divide, tax law, EU tax law, EU law, class actions

Suggested Citation:

Mitchell, Charles, Recovery of Overpaid Tax Under English Law (October 1, 2016). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2847060 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.2847060
