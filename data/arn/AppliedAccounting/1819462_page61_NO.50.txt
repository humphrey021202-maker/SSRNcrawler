Limited Liability of Auditors: An Analysis of Caparo Industries plc v. Dickman

32 Pages Posted: 24 Apr 2011

Saswat Subasit

Symbiosis International University

Shatabdi Patnaik

Symbiosis International University

Date Written: April 22, 2011

Abstract

This is a case comment on the famous House of Lords judgment in Caparo Industries plc v. Dickman which specified the law on limited liability of auditors in the United Kingdom.

Keywords: Caparo Industries, Dickman, limited liability, auditors, House of Lords

Suggested Citation:

Subasit, Saswat and Patnaik, Shatabdi, Limited Liability of Auditors: An Analysis of Caparo Industries plc v. Dickman (April 22, 2011). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1819462 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.1819462
