Rethinking the Penalty for the Failure to File Gift Tax Returns

Tax Notes, Vol. 141, No. 7, 2013

Rutgers School of Law-Newark Research Paper No. 137

Pepperdine University Legal Studies Research Paper No. 2014/2

6 Pages Posted: 6 Dec 2013 Last revised: 5 Mar 2014

Jay A. Soled

Rutgers University

Paul L. Caron

Pepperdine University - Rick J. Caruso School of Law

Charles Davenport

Rutgers University School of Law

Richard Schmalbeck

Duke University School of Law

Date Written: November 18, 2013

Abstract

In this article, the authors argue that Congress must reform the penalty structure associated with the failure to file gift tax returns if it wants to maintain the integrity of the transfer tax system.

Keywords: gift tax, penalty

Suggested Citation:

Soled, Jay and Caron, Paul L. and Davenport, Charles and Schmalbeck, Richard L., Rethinking the Penalty for the Failure to File Gift Tax Returns (November 18, 2013). Tax Notes, Vol. 141, No. 7, 2013, Rutgers School of Law-Newark Research Paper No. 137, Pepperdine University Legal Studies Research Paper No. 2014/2, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2363478
