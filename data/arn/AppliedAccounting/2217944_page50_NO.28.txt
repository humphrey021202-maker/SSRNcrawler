Statutory and Effective Tax Rates: Part 2

Tax Notes, Vol. 137, No. 3, 2012

5 Pages Posted: 14 Feb 2013

Alan D. Viard

American Enterprise Institute

Date Written: October 15, 2012

Abstract

In the second article of a two-part series, Viard discusses the work incentive effects of capital income taxes and reforms that replace income taxation with consumption taxation.
