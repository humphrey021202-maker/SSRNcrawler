Beyond the Ivory Tower: Sharing Accounting Research with Non-Academics

7 Pages Posted: 12 Apr 2021 Last revised: 15 Aug 2022

Judith Hermis

St. Mary's College of California

Date Written: August 14, 2022

Abstract

This paper explores the need for accounting researchers to share our work outside the academy, the means for us to do so, and obstacles currently preventing such widespread knowledge sharing.

Keywords: Accounting research, practitioners, research crisis

JEL Classification: M40, M41, M48, M49

Suggested Citation:

Hermis, Judith, Beyond the Ivory Tower: Sharing Accounting Research with Non-Academics (August 14, 2022). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3823360 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3823360
