South Africa's New Real Estate Investment Trust Legislation

Posted: 20 Mar 2024

Ernest Mazansky

Werksmans Tax, Limited

Date Written: February 25, 2013

Abstract

In this article, the author describes South Africa's new Real Estate Investment Trust regime and its tax implications.
