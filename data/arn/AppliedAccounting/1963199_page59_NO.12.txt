The McKesson Case: Key Transfer Pricing Principles in the Context of Debt Factoring

Transfer Pricing International Journal, 2011

3 Pages Posted: 22 Nov 2011 Last revised: 25 Jul 2012

Rupert Macey-Dare

St Cross College - University of Oxford; Middle Temple; Minerva Chambers

Danny Beeton

Freshfields

Date Written: November 22, 2011

Abstract

This paper discusses the current trial of McKesson Canada Corporation v. The Queen in the Tax Court of Canada and the related-party debt factoring arrangement and transfer pricing issues between McKesson Canada Corporation and McKesson International Holdings III S.A. R.L, a company resident in Luxembourg.

Keywords: transfer pricing, OECD, factoring, arm's length

JEL Classification: H25, H20, H26, H87

Suggested Citation:

Macey-Dare, Rupert and Beeton, Danny, The McKesson Case: Key Transfer Pricing Principles in the Context of Debt Factoring (November 22, 2011). Transfer Pricing International Journal, 2011, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1963199 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.1963199
