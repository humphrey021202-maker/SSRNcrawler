Taxation on the Economic Exploitation of Athlete Image Rights – How it Works

Consultor Jurídico, 2016

7 Pages Posted: 20 Oct 2016

Rafael Pandolfo

Brazilian Institute of Tax Studies (IBET)

Date Written: May 21, 2016

Abstract

The present article seeks to study about the taxation on the economic exploitation of athlete image rights. How it works according to Brazilian legal tax system, the nature of this type of right and the possible ways to exploit it.

Keywords: Brazilian tax system, Athlete Image Rights

Suggested Citation:

Pandolfo, Rafael, Taxation on the Economic Exploitation of Athlete Image Rights – How it Works (May 21, 2016). Consultor Jurídico, 2016, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2854206
