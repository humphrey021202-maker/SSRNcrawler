A Brief Review of Corporate Tax Articles of 2013

Tax Notes, Vol. 143, No. 11, 2014

7 Pages Posted: 16 Aug 2014

Jordan M. Barry

University of Southern California Gould School of Law

Karen C. Burke

University of Florida Levin College of Law

Monica Gianni

California State University Northridge

Date Written: June 16, 2014

Abstract

This article provides a short survey of corporate tax articles published in 2013, with the goal of bringing readers' attention to articles that may be of interest but might have escaped their notice.

Keywords: corporate tax

JEL Classification: H26

Suggested Citation:

Barry, Jordan and Burke, Karen C. and Gianni, Monica, A Brief Review of Corporate Tax Articles of 2013 (June 16, 2014). Tax Notes, Vol. 143, No. 11, 2014, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2480514
