Deferred Income Tax (Odložená Daň Z Príjmov)

Učtovnicto, Auditorstvo, Daňovnictvo, 2000

Posted: 21 Nov 2011

Piotr W. Staszkiewicz

Warsaw School of Economics

Lucia Bencikova

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: May 1, 2000

Abstract

The paper discusses the application of the accounting for deferred tax in Slovak accounting environment. Risk analysis and audit treatments has been illustrated with simplified case study.

Keywords: Accounting, Tax, Deferred tax, IAS, SK GAAP

JEL Classification: M40, M41

Suggested Citation:

Staszkiewicz, Piotr Wojciech and Bencikova, Lucia, Deferred Income Tax (Odložená Daň Z Príjmov) (May 1, 2000). Učtovnicto, Auditorstvo, Daňovnictvo, 2000, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1962341
