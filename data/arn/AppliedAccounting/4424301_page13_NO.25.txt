The Development of the Cost Accounting Theory in the Russian Accounting System

4 Pages Posted: 2 May 2023

Anna Vekshina

Moscow State Institute of International Relations (MGIMO)

Date Written: April 21, 2023

Abstract

The article examines the key provisions of the Russian accounting science in terms of cost accounting and prime cost calculation. The question of the relationship between cost accounting and prime cost calculation is discussed, and the correlation between the category’s "costs", "charges", "expenses" and "prime cost" is also established.

Keywords: cost, accounting, cost management, charges, prime cost calculation

JEL Classification: D22, M41, M49

Suggested Citation:
