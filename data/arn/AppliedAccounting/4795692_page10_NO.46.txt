Slicing the Pie in Latin America : A Pragmatic Approach to a Value Chain Analysis

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 12 ; p. 718-731; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/27xn2jm

Posted: 28 May 2024

Steef Huibregtse

TPA Global

Sonia Catalina Muñoz Rodríguez

TaxModel International

Date Written: November 12, 2018

Abstract

This article considers the use of a value chain analysis in a Latin American context, with an emphasis on the adoption of a pragmatic approach to the use of this technique with regard to transfer pricing issues.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: value chain analysis, transfer pricing, collection of tax

JEL Classification: K33, K34

Suggested Citation:
