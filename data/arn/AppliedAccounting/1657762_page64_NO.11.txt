EVA: A Radical Fix to Broken Accounting

4 Pages Posted: 13 Aug 2010

Ray Titus

Alliance Business School

Date Written: August 12, 2010

Abstract

This article explores the concept of Economic profit is a better measure of corporate performance as it considers all the costs of resources used in generating particular revenue.

Keywords: EVA, Economic Profit, Accounting

Suggested Citation:

Titus, Ray, EVA: A Radical Fix to Broken Accounting (August 12, 2010). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1657762 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.1657762
