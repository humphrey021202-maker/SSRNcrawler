The Commission’s Proposal To Introduce an EU Financial Transaction Tax

European Taxation, No. 2/3, pp. 77-84, 2012

Posted: 27 Feb 2012

Benjamin Serafin Cortez

University of Hohenheim

Thorsten Vogel

University of Hohenheim

Date Written: February 1, 2012

Abstract

This article provides insight into the current discussions regarding the implementation of a financial transaction tax in the European Union, in particular the Commission’s proposal to introduce an EU FTT.

Keywords: financial transaction tax, FTT, Finanztransaktionssteuer, Tobin tax, taxation financial transactions

Suggested Citation:
