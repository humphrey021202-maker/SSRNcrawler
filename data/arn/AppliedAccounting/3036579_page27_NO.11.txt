Core of HR Strategy: System Thinking and HRM

Uysal, G. (2017). Core of HR Strategy. Management Studies.

4 Pages Posted: 16 Sep 2017

Gürhan Uysal

Ondokuz Mayıs University; Ondokuz Mays University

There are 3 versions of this paper

Date Written: September 13, 2017

Abstract

Core of HR Strategy is system thinking. In American HRM HPWS represents system approach. It is believed HPWS has impact on performance of employees, and it has impact on organisational performance.

Keywords: HR, System, HPWS

JEL Classification: P5, G20
