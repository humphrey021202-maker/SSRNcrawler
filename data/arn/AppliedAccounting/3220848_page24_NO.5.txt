Public Country-by-Country Reporting: The Shareholders’ Case for Mandatory Disclosure

Tax Notes International, Vol. 90, No. 14, 2018

11 Pages Posted: 30 Aug 2018

Noam Noked

The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) - Faculty of Law

Date Written: June 25, 2018

Abstract

This article argues that mandatory disclosure of country-by-country reports is in the best interest of the shareholders of publicly traded multinational enterprises. It urges regulators to act quickly as tax authorities begin exchanging reports.

Keywords: Country-by-Country Reporting, CbCR, Mandatory Disclosure, Tax Transparency, BEPS, Public Shareholders

JEL Classification: K22, K34

Suggested Citation:
