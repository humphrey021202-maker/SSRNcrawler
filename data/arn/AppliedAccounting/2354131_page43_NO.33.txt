Same-Sex Marriage and Estate Taxes: Why Windsor is Still at Issue

Tax Notes, Vol. 141, No. 1, 2013

6 Pages Posted: 15 Nov 2013

David Herzig

Valparaiso University Law School; Ernst & Young

Date Written: October 7, 2013

Abstract

In this article, Herzig discusses the estate and gift tax implications of United States v. Windsor.

Keywords: Windsor, Rev. Rul. 2013-17, DOMA, same sex marriage, estate tax, gift tax, 2056, Chenoweth

JEL Classification: H2, H20, K34
