Let's Play Fair: Reconsidering the U.S. Corporate Tax System

Tax Notes International, Vol. 114, No. 5, 2024

4 Pages Posted: 10 Jun 2024

Roy Clemons

New Mexico State University

Date Written: April 29, 2024

Abstract

In this article, Clemons argues that the United States should replace its quasi-territorial tax system and proposes a framework that taxes domestic and multinational corporations at the same statutory tax rate on a country-by-country basis.

Suggested Citation:

Clemons, Roy, Let's Play Fair: Reconsidering the U.S. Corporate Tax System (April 29, 2024). Tax Notes International, Vol. 114, No. 5, 2024, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4823996
