Corporate Liability Under the FCPA: Identifying Defense Opportunities

William G. Carpenter & Thomas P. Stutsman, Corporate Liability under the FCPA: Identifying Defense Opportunities, BENCH & BAR OF MINNESOTA, July 2014, at 24-28.

7 Pages Posted: 28 Jul 2014

William Carpenter

Briol & Associates, PLLC

Thomas Stutsman

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: July 1, 2014

Abstract

This article analyzes the Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA) enforcement action against Archer Daniels Midland Company to illustrate defense opportunities in FCPA cases.

Keywords: Foreign Corrupt Practices Act; FCPA; White-Collar Crime

Suggested Citation:

Carpenter, William and Stutsman, Thomas Patrick, Corporate Liability Under the FCPA: Identifying Defense Opportunities (July 1, 2014). William G. Carpenter & Thomas P.
