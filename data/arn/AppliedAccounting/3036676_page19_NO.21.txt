Can't Get There from Here: The IRS Travel Ban on Tax Debtors

Bloomberg BNA Tax Management Memorandum, 2017

10 Pages Posted: 21 Apr 2020

Anson Asbury

Asbury Law Firm

Date Written: September 11, 2017

Abstract

An overview of IRC Section 7345 which allows the IRS to restrict and revoke passports for taxpayers with seriously delinquent tax debts. The article discusses the interplay between the provisions of the statute and the existing IRS collections regime.

Note: “Reproduced with permission from Tax Management Memorandum, 2017. Copyright 2017 by The Bureau of National Affairs, Inc. tel: 1-800-372-1033 http://www.bna.com.”

Keywords: Passport Revocation, IRS Collections, Collection Due Process, Passport Suspension, Tax Debt,
