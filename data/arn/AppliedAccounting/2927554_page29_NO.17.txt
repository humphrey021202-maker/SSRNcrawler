FASB and Guarantees of Variable Interest Entities

Tax Notes, Vol. 154, No. 4, 2017

U of Texas Law, Public Law Research Paper

5 Pages Posted: 6 Mar 2017

Calvin H. Johnson

University of Texas at Austin - School of Law

Date Written: January 23, 2017

Abstract

Johnson proposes that the Financial Accounting Standards Board stop allowing companies to report revenue from the debt of variable interest entities that the companies have guaranteed.

Suggested Citation:

Johnson, Calvin Harsha, FASB and Guarantees of Variable Interest Entities (January 23, 2017). Tax Notes, Vol. 154, No. 4, 2017, U of Texas Law, Public Law Research Paper, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2927554
