IFRS 17 – No Time to Waste

2 Pages Posted: 4 Oct 2022

Michael Winkler

Shasat Consulting

Sunil Kansal

Shasat Consulting

Date Written: August 25, 2022

Abstract

The objective of this article is to highlight some of the key changes because of the transition from IFRS 4 to IFRS 17 and how the financial information presented by the insurance companies will transform from 1 January 2023.

Keywords: Accounting, reinsurance, IFRS, IFRS17, Insurance, Shasat, IFRS Courses, reinsurance, Actuarial, Actuary, IASB, FASB, K-IFRS1117, NIIF

JEL Classification: G22, G24, G34, N2, G10, G11, G12, G15, G28, E58, N20, F65, G32, G21, G24, G14, G01, F23, F15, Z23

Suggested Citation:
