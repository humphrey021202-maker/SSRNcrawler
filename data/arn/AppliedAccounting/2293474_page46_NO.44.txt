IRS Provides Important Guidance for Inbound Investments in REITs

Tax Notes, Vol. 129, No. 215, 2010

8 Pages Posted: 15 Jul 2013

Seth J. Entin

Greenberg Traurig

Date Written: October 11, 2010

Abstract

This article analyzes an IRS private letter ruling that provides important clarification about when stock in a real estate investment trust qualifies for the domestically controlled qualified investment entity exception from taxation under the 1980 Foreign Investment in Real Property Tax Act, also known as FIRPTA.

Keywords: FIRPTA, REIT, foreign, investors, U.S. real estate, tax, capital gains, structure

JEL Classification: K34
