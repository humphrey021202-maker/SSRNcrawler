Primer on Qualified Opportunity Zones

Primer on Qualified Opportunity Zones, Tax Notes, Vol. 159, No. 7, 945-972 (May 14, 2018)

44 Pages Posted: 9 Jan 2019

Adam Wallwork

Deloitte Tax LLP; Deloitte Tax LP; Deloitte Tax LP

Linda Schakel

Ballard Spahr LLP

Date Written: May 14, 2018

Abstract

In this report, Wallwork and Schakel describe the nomination and designation process for qualified opportunity zones (QOZs) and how investors can profit from investments held in QOZ businesses held by qualified opportunity funds. They also discuss the mechanics of gain exclusion under the QOZ provisions.


Note: Adam S. Wallwork and Linda B. Schakel are attorneys in the tax and public finance groups at Ballard Spahr LLP.

Keywords: tax, tax cuts and jobs act, opportunity zones, qualified opportunity zones, qualified opportunity funds

JEL Classification: K34, O16
