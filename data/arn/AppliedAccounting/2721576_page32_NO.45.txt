Intractable Tax Problems? Consider a Congressional Referral

Taxpro Journal, Fall 2015; Vol. 22 (3) ISSN 1536-5875‎

4 Pages Posted: 27 Jan 2016

Charles Markham, MST, EA, USTCP

Markham & Company LLC

Date Written: July 25, 2015

Abstract

Sometimes when a practitioner is representing a taxpayer, they may run into an intractable tax problem. This article outlines making use of a congressional referral a technique that should be used very sparingly to resolve your client's tax long standing tax problem.

Keywords: Congressional referral, casework, taxpayer advocate, taxpayer representation

JEL Classification: K34

Suggested Citation:
