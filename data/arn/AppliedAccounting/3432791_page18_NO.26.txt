Debt-Financed Distributions from QOF Partnerships

Tax Notes Federal, 2019

7 Pages Posted: 20 Jul 2020

Islame Hosny

Columbia University; Pryor Cashman LLP

Date Written: June 24, 2019

Abstract

This article examines the effect of debt-financed distributions on the 10-year basis step-up of investments in qualified opportunity fund partnerships. The article explores possible outcomes under relevant Treasury and IRS guidance, discusses the related policy implications, and makes recommendations for future guidance.

Keywords: qualified opportunity fund, qualified opportunity zone, debt-financed distribution, tax

Suggested Citation:
