Evaluation of Various Methods of Accounting for Employee Stock Options

17 Pages Posted: 18 Jan 2016

Pavak Vyas

Ahmedabad University

Date Written: August 1, 2007

Abstract

With development of capital markets and evolution of IT and ITeS companies the trend of granting stock options as part of remuneration package gained usage and importance. This paper deals with the Evolution of Employee Stock Options, various ways of accounting of stock options granted to employees as remuneration and evaluation of such methods of valuation of such stock options, global perspective thereon and the Indian practice.

Keywords: ESOP, Vesting

JEL Classification: M41
