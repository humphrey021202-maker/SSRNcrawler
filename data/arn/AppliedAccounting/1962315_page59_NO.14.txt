Jubilee and Retirement Provision (Jubilejné Odmeny a Odstupné)

Učtovnicto, Auditorstvo, Daňovnictvo, Forthcoming

Posted: 21 Nov 2011

Piotr W. Staszkiewicz

Warsaw School of Economics

Lucia Staszkiewicz

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: November 20, 2000

Abstract

The paper presents the methods for auditing and disclosing the jubilee and retirements provision. The accounting treatment and risk assessment is presented. A simplified example illustrates the discussed issue.

Keywords: Actuarial provision, Risk accounting, SK GAAP

JEL Classification: M40, M41

Suggested Citation:

Staszkiewicz, Piotr Wojciech and Staszkiewicz, Lucia, Jubilee and Retirement Provision (Jubilejné Odmeny a Odstupné) (November 20, 2000). Učtovnicto, Auditorstvo, Daňovnictvo, Forthcoming , Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1962315
