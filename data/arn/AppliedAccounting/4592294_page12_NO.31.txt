Grantor Trust Ownership: What Does It Mean?

TAX NOTES FEDERAL, APRIL 13, 2020

7 Pages Posted: 18 Oct 2023

Monte Jackel

Jackel Tax Law; Jackel Medium.com

Date Written: April 4, 2020

Abstract

In this article, Jackel examines grantor trust ownership and its effect on the federal tax separate-entity status of the trust.

Keywords: tax; grantor trusts
