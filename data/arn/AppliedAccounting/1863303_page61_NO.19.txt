Tax Policy Encourages Home Ownership

17 Pages Posted: 13 Jun 2011

R. Wayne Saubert

Radford University

Lynn Saubert

Radford University

Dan Davidson

Radford University - Department of Accounting, Finance & Business Law

Date Written: 2011

Abstract

This paper examines the tax code provisions that encourage home ownership, including a discussion of some new legislation that also falls within the current administration’s economic stimulus package. It reviews tax provisions dealing with buying a home, owning a home, and selling a home, as well as tax credits and special treatment of some expenses.

Keywords: tax policy, home ownership, tax credit

JEL Classification: D1, H2, H22, H23, H24, H81, K34, M4

Suggested Citation:

Saubert, R. Wayne and Saubert, Lynn and Davidson, Dan, Tax Policy Encourages Home Ownership (2011). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1863303 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.1863303
