Anti-Tax Avoidance Measures in Lithuanian Case-Law

Posted: 14 Jan 2015

Mantas Juozaitis

Motieka & Audzevicius

Date Written: September 2, 2013

Abstract

This is the first detailed analysis of the case-law of Lithuanian courts in the tax field related to the application of anti-avoidance measures. It deals with CFC, Thin capitalization, Transfer pricing, Substance over form issues. The author highlights various contradictions in the national case law and the one of the CJEU. The paper deals with both direct tax and VAT.

Keywords: Anti-avoidance, Substance over form, CFC, Thin capitalization, Thin cap, Lithuania, tax, tax law, CJEU, ECJ, Step transaction, VAT

Suggested Citation:

Juozaitis, Mantas, Anti-Tax Avoidance Measures in Lithuanian Case-Law (September 2, 2013). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2546736
