Draft Circular Sheds Light on Tax Ramifications of Italian Taxation of Trusts

European Taxation: Vol. 62 (2022), Issue 8, Pages 360-362

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Alberto Trainotti

Independent

S. Fabbiano

Independent

Abstract

This note examines the tax treatment of trusts in Italy in light of a draft Circular of the Italian tax authorities, published in August 2021, on Italian tax provisions related to trusts.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1s5kjkv

Keywords: trust, tax authorities, direct tax, indirect tax, gift tax, wealth tax

Suggested Citation:
