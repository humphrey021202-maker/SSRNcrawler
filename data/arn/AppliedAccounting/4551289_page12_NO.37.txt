Six Principles for the Effective Use of ChatGPT and other Large Language Models in Accounting

CPA Journal (Forthcoming)

20 Pages Posted: 27 Aug 2023

Daniel Street

Bucknell University

Joseph Wilck

Bucknell University

Zachariah Chism

University of Alabama - Culverhouse School of Accountancy

Date Written: August 24, 2023

Abstract

Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT can improve the efficiency of common language generation tasks performed by CPAs, but using LLMs creates certain risks. To help CPAs reap the benefits of LLMs while minimizing the risks of this emerging technology we suggest nine principles to guide CPAs as they incorporate LLMs into their work.

Keywords: ChatGPT, Large Language Models, Chatbots, AI

JEL Classification: M4, M15, M40, M41, M42

Suggested Citation:
