The Case for a 'Super-Matching' Rule

Tax Law Review, Vol. 65, p. 242, 2012

72 Pages Posted: 22 May 2012

Yaron Z. Reich

affiliation not provided to SSRN

There are 2 versions of this paper

Date Written: May 4, 2012

Abstract

Tax Law Review article (Volume 65, No. 2) on the consistency (matching) concept in the tax law, and how mismatches in the treatment of offsetting and other related items might be addressed by Congress, the Treasury and the IRS.

Keywords: consistency, matching

JEL Classification: Tax

Suggested Citation:

Reich, Yaron Z., The Case for a 'Super-Matching' Rule (May 4, 2012). Tax Law Review, Vol. 65, p. 242, 2012, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=2064944
