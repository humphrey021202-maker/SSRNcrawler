Costing Assignment Approaches

8 Pages Posted: 27 Jul 2018 Last revised: 4 Aug 2018

Edwin Triyuwono

Atma Jaya Makassar University

Fadly Chandra

Atma Jaya Makassar University

Marselinus Asri

Atma Jaya Makassar University

Date Written: July 7, 2018

Abstract

This paper aims to review the costing assignment approaches in managerial accounting. These approaches are widely used in manufacturing companies with exception of actual costing since it’s very hard to applied to real world setting. The paper begin with the review of the actual costing, normal costing, and standard costing each with its unique characteristics and features. Finally, the paper will compare normal costing and standard costing, whichever are better to applied.

Keywords: Costing, Cost Assignment, Actual Costing, Normal Costing, Standard Costing

Suggested Citation:
