Actuarial Challenges and IFRS 17

5 Pages Posted: 30 Sep 2020 Last revised: 20 Jul 2021

Michael Winkler

Shasat Consulting

Sunil Kansal

Shasat Consulting

Date Written: August 10, 2020

Abstract

The objective of this write up is to highlight some of the critical areas key to the implementation of IFRS 17 and also ensuring that businesses can align with or reflect commercial practices in their accounting.

Keywords: IFRS 17, Actuarial, Insurance, Accounting, reinsurance

JEL Classification: G22, G24, G34, N2, G10, G11, G12, G15, G28, E58, N20

Suggested Citation:

Winkler, Michael and Kansal, Sunil, Actuarial Challenges and IFRS 17 (August 10, 2020). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3670808 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3670808
