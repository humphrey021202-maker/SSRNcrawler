Systemic Issues with Low-Dollar Lien Filing

Tax Notes, Vol. 133, No. 1, October 2011

Villanova Law/Public Policy Research Paper No. 2011-20

11 Pages Posted: 19 Nov 2011 Last revised: 22 Dec 2011

Keith Fogg

Harvard Law School; Tax Clinic at Legal Services Center of Harvard Law School

Date Written: October 1, 2011

Abstract

This article suggests a better system for exercising judgment when filing the notice of federal tax lien on low dollar delinquent accounts.

Keywords: federal tax lien, collection due process, withdrawal of the notice of federal tax lien

Suggested Citation:

Fogg, Keith, Systemic Issues with Low-Dollar Lien Filing (October 1, 2011). Tax Notes, Vol. 133, No. 1, October 2011, Villanova Law/Public Policy Research Paper No. 2011-20, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1961695
