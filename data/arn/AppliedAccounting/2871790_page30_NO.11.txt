Roth Retirement Accounts: A Practitioner's Approach

CCH Journal of Retirement Planning September/October 2008

9 Pages Posted: 22 Nov 2016 Last revised: 31 Aug 2020

Mark Mercer

Independent

Date Written: October 31, 2008

Abstract

Mark Mercer describes the factors that must be considered when choosing between a traditional IRA and a Roth IRA.

Keywords: roth ira traditional versus conversion tax
