FOUNDATIONS OF FINTECH: NAVIGATING THE DIGITAL FINANCIAL REVOLUTION

217 Pages Posted: 12 Feb 2025 Last revised: 12 Feb 2025

Arun Adwani

Coventry University; The Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA); Chartered Professional Accountants; Oxford Brookes University

Date Written: January 28, 2025

Abstract

The world of finance is undergoing a profound transformation, driven by the disruptive power of technology. The advent of financial technology, or fintech, has reshaped how individuals, businesses, and institutions interact with money, paving the way for innovative solutions that redefine traditional financial systems. Foundations of Fintech: Navigating the Digital Financial Revolution provides a comprehensive exploration of this digital transformation, offering readers an in-depth understanding of the technologies, trends, and challenges shaping the future of finance.
