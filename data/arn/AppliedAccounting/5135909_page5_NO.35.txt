Occupational Health and Safety Disclosure Practices during the COVID-19 Pandemic

The 8th Equal Opportunities Conference “Digital Innovation and Business Sustainability” (EO2025), Ahlia University, Kingdom of Bahrain, 12th -13th February 2025. 

6 Pages Posted: 10 Apr 2025 Last revised: 20 May 2025

Alaa Abudayah

University of Sharjah

Danah Alamodi

University of Sharjah

Walaa Elkelish

University of Sharjah

Aziz Jaafar

University of Sharjah

Date Written: February 12, 2025

Abstract

This paper explores the extent of health and safety disclosure practices and their influence on employees' and firms' performances during the COVID-19 pandemic. This paper collects primary qualitative data using semi-structured interviews. Using the content analysis, the findings show vital relationships between health and safety disclosure and employees' and firms' performance. Several challenges related to health and safety disclosure practices include working conditions, legislation, and medical information privacy. This paper increases our understanding of health and safety disclosure practices during the COVID-19 pandemic for policy improvements.

Keywords: Health and safety, disclosure, performance, COVID-19

JEL Classification: M41, M48
