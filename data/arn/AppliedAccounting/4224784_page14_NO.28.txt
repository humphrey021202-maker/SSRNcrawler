Planning Around Excess Basis in Corporate Formation Transfers

176 Tax Notes Fed. 1209 (2022)

12 Pages Posted: 28 Sep 2022 Last revised: 15 Oct 2025

Melanie McCoskey

University of Akron - The George W. Daverio School of Accountancy

Doron Narotzki

University of Akron - The George W. Daverio School of Accountancy

Date Written: August 22, 2022

Abstract

In this article, McCoskey and Narotzki explain why the basis rules under section 358 require tweaking when, in exchange for stock, a transferor of property also receives an installment note with excess basis.

Keywords: Federal Tax, Corporate Tax, Tax Planning, Tax Policy, Tax

JEL Classification: K00, K1, K34

Suggested Citation:

McCoskey, Melanie and Narotzki, Doron, Planning Around Excess Basis in Corporate Formation Transfers (August 22, 2022). 176 Tax Notes Fed. 1209 (2022) , Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=4224784
