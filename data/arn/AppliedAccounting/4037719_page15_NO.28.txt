Transfers of Zero-Basis Intangibles to a Partnership

174 Tax Notes 5 (Jan. 3, 2022)

University of Florida Levin College of Law Research Paper no. 22-16

13 Pages Posted: 23 Feb 2022 Last revised: 18 Apr 2022

Karen C. Burke

University of Florida Levin College of Law

Date Written: December 3, 2021

Abstract

The article examines the problem of contributed zero-basis intangibles in light of the IRS’s inadvertent disclosure of a transaction structured by Bristol-Myers Squibb to shift billions of dollars of built-in gain to a related foreign partner.

Keywords: partnership, foreign, intangibles, built-in gain

JEL Classification: K34, H24, H25, H26

Suggested Citation:
