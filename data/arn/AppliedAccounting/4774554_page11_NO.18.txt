Capital Gains Tax for Non-Residents Comes Under Significant Review

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 69 (2015), no. 2 ; p. 124-127; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/78tg0

Posted: 29 Mar 2024

Anton Joseph

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: January 21, 2015

Abstract

In this article, the author examines the Australian rules on the taxation of capital gains derived by non-residents on disposal of Australian real property.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: capital gains tax, non-resident, disposal, immovable property, withholding tax, tax treaty

JEL Classification: K33, K34
