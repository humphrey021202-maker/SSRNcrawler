The Organization of the Multinational Firm - Perspectives on Global Transfer Pricing

Posted: 25 May 2011

Markus Brem

GTP GlobalTransferPricing Business Solutions GmbH; University of Augsburg

Thomas Alexander Tucha

SAVYVA GmbH

Date Written: May 12, 2011

Abstract

MNEs have significantly changed their way of doing business such as from "a parent runs its distant foreign sourcing or distribution subsidiaries" into a more "global organization operating multi-cultural, multi-national business lines and value chains".

Keywords: Global Transfer Pricing, Multinational Firm

Suggested Citation:

Brem, Markus and Tucha, Thomas Alexander, The Organization of the Multinational Firm - Perspectives on Global Transfer Pricing (May 12, 2011). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1839306 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.1839306
