The Impact of the Right to a Fair Trial on Tax Evidence: An EU Analysis

HUMAN RIGHTS AND TAXATION IN EUROPE AND THE WORLD, p. 489, G. Kofler, M. Poiares Maduro & P. Pistone, eds., IBFD, 2011

18 Pages Posted: 28 Feb 2012

Gianluigi Bizioli

University of Bergamo - Department of Law

Date Written: February 15, 2011

Abstract

This contribution analyzes the impact of the right to a fair trial on tax proceedings. In particular, it deals with the role of human rights and fundamental principles within the EU order.

Keywords: human rights, taxation, right to a fair trial, tax evidence

JEL Classification: K19, K33, K34

Suggested Citation:
