Ratio Analysis of Emaar

10 Pages Posted: 11 Jun 2020

Mah-Noor Bint-Tariq

Abu Dhabi University

Ahmed Al Dhaheri

Abu Dhabi University

Alia AlMazrouie

Abu Dhabi University

Laila Al-Blooshi

Abu Dhabi University

Fatima Al Mazrouei

Abu Dhabi University

Haitham Nobanee

Abu Dhabi University; University of Oxford; University of Liverpool

Date Written: May 17, 2020

Abstract

This project aims to determine the performance of Emaar Properties PJSC using its financial statements for the year 2018 and 2019. The research shows various calculations and explanations to determine the performance of Emaar Properties PJSC. After calculating Liquidity Ratios, Asset Management Ratios or Turnover or Efficiency ratios, Profitability Ratios, and Solvency Ratios through this project we concluded that investors should invest in Emaar Properties PJSC.

Suggested Citation:

Bint-Tariq, Mah-Noor and Al Dhaheri, Ahmed and AlMazrouie, Alia and Al-Blooshi, Laila and Al Mazrouei, Fatima and Nobanee, Haitham, Ratio Analysis of Emaar (May 17, 2020). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3603270 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3603270
