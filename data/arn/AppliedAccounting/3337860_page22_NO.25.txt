O'Hagan, 10b-5, Relationships and Duties

Hastings Business Law Journal, Vol. 4, No. 1, 2008

23 Pages Posted: 25 Feb 2019

Thomas M. Madden

Marist College School of Management

Date Written: February 1, 2008

Abstract

Analysis of Rule 10b5-2 and the development of the misappropriation theory of insider or outsider trading via the Supreme Court O'Hagan decision.

Keywords: securities, securities law, fraud, Rule 10b-5, rule 10b5-2, securities fraud, misappropriation theory, insider trading, outsider trading

Suggested Citation:
