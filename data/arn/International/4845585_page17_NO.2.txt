Australian Residence: Time to Change the Rules?

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 5 ; 6 p.; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3tc5sy3

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

Anton Joseph

Independent

Date Written: April 8, 2019

Abstract

The corporate and individual residence rules in Australia have remained unchanged for a long time, despite the significant transformations that have taken place regarding how companies are structured and the increased mobility of labour. This article argues that it is now high time that Australia changed these rules.

Keywords: residence, central management and control, domicile, case law, ruling
