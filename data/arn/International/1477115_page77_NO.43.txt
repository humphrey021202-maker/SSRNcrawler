Progressive Taxation or Single Quota?

Posted: 23 Sep 2009

Elena Padurean

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Delia Catarama

affiliation not provided to SSRN

Date Written: September 22, 2009

Abstract

If we think of Romania, a country with a difficult administration, with multiple unnecessary bureaucracy, with a big tax evasion, not we can not try a comparison of the two types of taxation revenue, especially through the effects they have on taxpayer, but also on the state budget.
