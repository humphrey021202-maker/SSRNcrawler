International Taxation and Multi-Level Governance

HANDBOOK ON MULTILEVEL GOVERNANCE, Henrik Enderlein, Sonja Wältli, Michael Zürn, eds., Edward Elgar, 2010

16 Pages Posted: 15 Oct 2009 Last revised: 18 Feb 2011

Thomas Rixen

University of Bamberg - Department of Political Science

Date Written: February 16, 2011

Abstract

This paper gives an overview of the institutional arrangements of international taxation and shows that they can be understood as a structure of multi-level governance.

Keywords: international taxation, multi-level governance

Suggested Citation:

Rixen, Thomas, International Taxation and Multi-Level Governance (February 16, 2011). HANDBOOK ON MULTILEVEL GOVERNANCE, Henrik Enderlein, Sonja Wältli, Michael Zürn, eds., Edward Elgar, 2010, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=1488057
