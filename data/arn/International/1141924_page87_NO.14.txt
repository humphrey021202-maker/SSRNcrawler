Accounting Certification in Ukraine

11 Pages Posted: 8 Jun 2008

Robert W. McGee

Fayetteville State University - Department of Accounting

Date Written: May 2008

Abstract

The United States Agency for International Development (USAID) initiated a regional accounting certification program in the Russian language several years ago. It started in Central Asia and spread to Ukraine and a few other former Soviet republics several years later. Headquarters for the program later moved to Moscow. This chapter reports on the CAP/CIPA accounting certification program that has been going on in Ukraine.

Keywords: accounting, certification, Ukraine, CIPA, IFRS, transition economy, gender

JEL Classification: G15, G18, G30, I2, J16, J44, L84, M4, O52, P31
