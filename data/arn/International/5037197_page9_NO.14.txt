EU Public Country-by-Country Reporting

International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 29 (2022), Issue 3, Pages 153-160

Posted: 2 Dec 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Susanne Verloove

Independent

P. Hoving

Independent

R. Aviles Gutierrez

Independent

Abstract

This article provides an overview of the new obligations, risks and perhaps also opportunities that EU public country-by-country reporting (CbCR) entails for the large MNE groups subject to CbCR obligations.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/2b4fzg9

Keywords: transfer pricing, CbC Reporting, EU Directive, reporting requirements, risk assessment, MNE

Suggested Citation:

Verloove, Susanne and Hoving, P. and Aviles Gutierrez, R., EU Public Country-by-Country Reporting. International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 29 (2022), Issue 3, Pages 153-160, Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=5037197 or http://dx.doi.org/10.59403/2b4fzg9
