Investment Incentives in Israel

Tax Notes International, Vol. 51, No. 5, 2008

5 Pages Posted: 12 May 2009

Avi Nov

College of Management (Israel) - School of Law; College of Management (Israel) - School of Business Administration

Date Written: May 10, 2009

Abstract

Companies operating in Israel, especially those held by foreign investors, may enjoy favorable tax incentives that can eliminate or substantially reduce the tax rates. This article outlines the various tax incentives granted under the Law for the Encouragement of Capital Investment, as well as the necessary requirements to enjoy these incentives.

Keywords: tax incentives, Israel, investment
