Analysis of a Worldwide Adoption of the International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS)

73 Pages Posted: 23 Nov 2013

Elshan Vakilov

University of Economics, Prague

Date Written: September 21, 2013

Abstract

The paper examines the worldwide adoption of IFRS - its history and culture and local factors in transition period.

Keywords: IFRS, EU, Russia and China
