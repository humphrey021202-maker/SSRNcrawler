Some Remarks About the Spanish Accountancy Legal Reform (Notas Sobre La Reforma Contable Española)

4 Pages Posted: 17 Jun 2008 Last revised: 22 Jul 2009

Anselmo M. Martinez Canellas

Universitat de les Illes Balears

Date Written: March 1, 2008

Abstract

The accounting regulations have been reformed in 2007 in Spain. These notes summarize the principal changes in the new General Accounting Plan and the specific accounting rules for small and medium enterprises.

Note: Downloadable document is in Spanish.

Keywords: accountancy in Spain

JEL Classification: M41, M44, M47

Suggested Citation:
