US Tax Cuts and Jobs Act: Part 2 - the Base Erosion and Anti-Abuse Tax (BEAT)

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 3 ; p. 154-165; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/khkf37

Posted: 4 Jun 2024

Carlos Pérez Gautrin

Independent

Date Written: February 6, 2019

Abstract

This article is the second in a series on US taxation that explores the recent US tax reform enacted by the US Tax Cuts and Jobs Act (TCJA) by focusing on Global Intangible Low-Taxed Income (GILTI), Foreign Derived Intangible Income (FDII) and the Base Erosion and Anti-Abuse Tax (BEAT). This article focusses on the BEAT.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: TCJA, BEAT, foreign tax credit, CFC, transfer pricing

JEL Classification: K33, K34
