The 2023 Portuguese State Budget: Are Families and Companies Sufficiently Protected Against Inflation?

European Taxation: Vol. 63 (2023), Issue 2/3, Pages 111-114

Posted: 22 Nov 2024 Last revised: 18 Feb 2025

Rita Botelho Moniz

Independent

Abstract

In this note, the author addresses the provisions of the 2023 Budget, most of which are aimed at mitigating the effects of inflation for companies and individuals.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/1zkcp1v

Keywords: budget law, inflation, individual income tax, brackets, deductions, cryptocurrency, withholding tax, corporate income tax, losses

Suggested Citation:
