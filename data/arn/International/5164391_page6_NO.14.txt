IT Sector in India

International Transfer Pricing Journal: Vol. 29 (2022), Issue 7

Posted: 4 Mar 2025

Vispi T. Patel

Independent

Kejal P. Savla

Independent

Anwesha Bandyopadhyay

Independent

Abstract

This article analyses the IT sector in India, the impact of the COVID-19 crisis and transfer pricing issues in the IT sector.

Full-text Paper: https://doi.org/10.59403/k271r1

Keywords: transfer pricing, information technology, COVID-19 pandemic, digital economy, R&D
