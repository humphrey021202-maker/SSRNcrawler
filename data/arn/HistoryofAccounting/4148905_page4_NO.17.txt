Chapter 12: Czech Republic (History and Taxation)

RADVAN, Michal, Petra JÁNOŠÍKOVÁ a Radim BOHÁČ. Chapter 12: Czech Republic. In Essers, P. (ed.). History and Taxation. 1. ed. Amsterdam: IBFD, 2022. pp. 221-241. EATLP International Tax Series, vol. 20. ISBN 978-90-8722-775-3.

Posted: 20 Jul 2022

Michal Radvan

Masaryk University - Faculty of Law

Petra Hrubá Smržová (Jánošíková)

Independent

Radim Boháč

Charles University in Prague - Faculty of Law

Date Written: June 28, 2022

Abstract

The chapter deals with the history of taxation in the Czech Republic. Particular attention is paid to repressive taxes during the Protectorate and Communism. Another area of interest for the authors are taxes supporting democracy and fiscal federalism.

Keywords: tax; tax law; repressive tax; democratic tax; fiscal federalism

JEL Classification: K34, N14
