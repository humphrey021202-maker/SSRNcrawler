Impact of the Tax Law on GAAP: 1909 to the 1950s 1

26 Pages Posted: 17 Dec 2024

Stephen A. Zeff

Rice University - Jesse H. Jones Graduate School of Business

Date Written: November 21, 2024

Abstract

The aim of this article is to bring to light and discuss the many and diverse impacts of the federal income tax law on generally accepted accounting principles, or GAAP. Such an analysis can provide accounting academics, practitioners, and standard setters with a broader perspective on these impacts on GAAP, focusing on the period from 1909 to the 1950s.

Keywords: income tax law, GAAP, income realization, depreciation, LIFO, book-tax conformity

Suggested Citation:
