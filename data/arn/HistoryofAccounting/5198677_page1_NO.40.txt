Taxation and the Founding Fathers

186 Tax Notes Fed. 1413 (2025).

33 Pages Posted: 17 Apr 2025 Last revised: 13 Oct 2025

Doron Narotzki

University of Akron - The George W. Daverio School of Accountancy

Tamir Shanan

College of Management

Julianne Jones

University of Akron - The George W. Daverio School of Accountancy

Date Written: February 18, 2025

Abstract

In this report, the authors examine the Founding Fathers' views and actions on taxes in Colonial America and the early United States, exploring the meaning of original intent in historical context and gleaning potential lessons for future U.S. tax policy.

Keywords: Tax, Legal History, Tax History, Colonial America, Colonial Tax, Founding Fathers, Federal Tax, Tariffs, Federalist Papers, The Distilled Spirit Tax of 1791, The 1794 Whiskey Rebellion, The Carriage Tax, The Window Tax, The Stamp Tax, Hylton

JEL Classification: K34

Suggested Citation:
