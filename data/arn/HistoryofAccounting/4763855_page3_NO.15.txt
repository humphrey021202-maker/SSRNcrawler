Radical Tax Reform for the 21st Century: The Role for a Consumption Tax

Wayne State University Law School Research Paper No. 4763855

Chapman Law Review, Forthcoming

26 Pages Posted: 20 Mar 2024

Alan S. Schenk

Wayne State University Law School

Date Written: March 18, 2024

Abstract

History of federal taxation in the 19th century and the role for consumption tax in the federal tax system, including federal proposals for VAT.

Keywords: value added tax, VAT, consumption tax, federal taxation in U.S., tax reform
