The New Context of the Accounting Profession

30 Pages Posted: 19 Sep 2017

Ahmed Riahi-Belkaoui

University of Illinois at Chicago - Department of Accounting

Date Written: September 15, 2017

Abstract

Using a structural perspective this working paper proposes that the new accounting environment includes four new dimensions in need of empirical investigations.

Keywords: Technical and ideological proletarianization, classwide rationality, manufactured consciousness, flawed universal class

Suggested Citation:

Riahi-Belkaoui, Ahmed, The New Context of the Accounting Profession (September 15, 2017). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3037598 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3037598
