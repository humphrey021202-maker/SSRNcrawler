Fairness in Business

46 Pages Posted: 13 Feb 2019

Ahmed Riahi-Belkaoui

University of Illinois at Chicago - Department of Accounting

Date Written: September 16, 2018

Abstract

The paper extends the concept of fairness from fairness in presentation to the more progressive notions of fairness in distribution, fairness in disclosure to motivate calls for expanded disclosures, and information innovations.

Keywords: fairness in presentation, fairness in distribution, fairness in disclosure

JEL Classification: A11

Suggested Citation:

Riahi-Belkaoui, Ahmed, Fairness in Business (September 16, 2018). Available at SSRN: https://ssrn.com/abstract=3250410 or http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3250410
