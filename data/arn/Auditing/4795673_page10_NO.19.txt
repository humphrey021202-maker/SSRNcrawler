The Potential of a Standard Audit File - Tax in European Union: A Chance for Coordinated VAT Administration?

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 72 (2018), no. 10 ; p. 582-594; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/1vd2sc0

Posted: 29 May 2024

Alicja Majdanska

Independent

Karol Dziwinski

Independent

Date Written: September 6, 2018

Abstract

In this article, the authors consider the potential for the introduction of a Standard Audit File - Tax (SAF-T) in the European Union with regard to VAT, and specifically whether this would offer opportunities to counter fraud and improve tax administration.

Full-text Paper

Keywords: SAF-T, audit, VAT, automation, accounting, electronic filing, subsidiarity, proportionality

JEL Classification: K33, K34
