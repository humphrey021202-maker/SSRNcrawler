Report of the Proceedings of the Ninth Assembly of the International Association of Tax Judges Held in Ottawa on 28 and 29 September 2018

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 73 (2019), no. 5 ; p. 267-285; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/parwj4

Posted: 3 Jun 2024

Bob Michel

Independent

Date Written: April 9, 2019

Abstract

This report summarizes the proceedings of the ninth Assembly of the International Association of Tax Judges, which was held in Ottawa on 28 and 29 September 2018. The following topics were covered: GAAR under BEPS and MLI; Precedent: stare decisis versus change of case law; Tax procedures in Canada; Recent case law on permanent establishments; Recent case law on tax fraud in VAT/GST; Transparency of court proceedings; and "Exotic" topic: "the most important court in Canada".

Full-text Paper

Keywords: GAAR, BEPS, MLI, case law, procedural tax law, PE, VAT fraud

JEL Classification: K33, K34
