The Impact of Technology on the Accounting Profession: A Comparative Study of Uzbekistan and Indonesia

5 Pages Posted: 13 May 2025

Sobirjon Khasatullaev

Institut Pendidikan Indonesia (IPI)

Date Written: March 10, 2025

Abstract

The accounting profession is undergoing a significant transformation due to advancements in technology. This article examines the emergence of cloud computing, artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning, and blockchain technology, highlighting their benefits and challenges. It also explores future trends shaping accounting practices, including automation, remote services, and sustainability reporting. A comparative analysis of Uzbekistan and Indonesia provides insights into how technology impacts accounting in both countries. Understanding these changes allows accounting professionals to adapt to the evolving landscape and enhance their value to clients and organizations.

Keywords: Technology, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, Uzbekistan, Indonesia, accounting, modern technology, blockchain, artificial intelligence, comparison between uzbekistan and indonesia
