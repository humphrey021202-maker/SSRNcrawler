Selling a Russian Business Can Be a Taxing Endeavour

Bulletin for International Taxation. - Amsterdam. - Vol. 71 (2017), no. 11 ; p. 649-653; DOI: https://doi.org/10.59403/3ttw7nf

Posted: 15 Apr 2024

Artem Vasyutin

Independent

Ekaterina Seryakova

Independent

Evgeniia Tkachenko

Independent

Date Written: October 18, 2017

Abstract

In this article, the authors consider controversial issues regarding the tax implications of selling companies owning real estate located in Russia, with special focus on the sale of Russian "real estate rich companies" (RERCs).

Full-text Paper

Keywords: immovable property, withholding tax, tax treaty

JEL Classification: K33, K34
